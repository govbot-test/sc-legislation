A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY ADDING SECTION 1-1-687 SO AS TO DESIGNATE "CAROLINA UNTIL I DIE" BY PATRICK DAVIS AS AN OFFICIAL STATE SONG. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Article 9, Chapter 1, Title 1 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: Section 1-1-687. "Carolina Until I Die" by Patrick Davis is designated as an official state song.

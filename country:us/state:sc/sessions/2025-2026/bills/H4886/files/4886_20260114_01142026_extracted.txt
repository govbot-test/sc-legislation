A HOUSE RESOLUTION to congratulate Julia Johnson of Lexington School District Four upon being named 2025 Sandhills middle school support staff member of the year, to express appreciation for her dedicated service, and to wish her continued success in the future. Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives are delighted to learn that Julia Johnson has been selected 2025 Support Staff Member of the Year for Sandhills Middle School in Lexington School District Four; and Whereas, each candidate for Support Staff Member of the Year undergoes a rigorous screening and assessment process in order to be selected for the award; and Whereas, these staff members act as role models and mentors for their colleagues and demonstrate a consistent commitment to excellence; and Whereas, such staff members possess a proven ability to make a positive difference for the district's students, schools, and communities; and Whereas, South Carolina is fortunate to have such dedicated and caring staff members supporting its teachers and administrators and helping to mold South Carolina's youth; and Whereas, Ms. Johnson has faithfully served as a custodian in Lexington School District Four for eighteen years, demonstrating an extraordinary work ethic and steadfast dedication to her duties, guided by her personal motto, "Nothing beats a hard day's work"; and Whereas, throughout her career, Ms. Johnson has gone above and beyond her responsibilities with integrity, care, and a spirit of service, earning the deep respect of colleagues and administrators alike; and Whereas, it is only fitting and proper that the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives recognize Julia Johnson for her exemplary service and commend her for making a meaningful and lasting impact on the lives of students and the broader school community. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, congratulate Julia Johnson upon being named 2025 Lexington School District Four Support Staff Member of the Year, express appreciation for her dedicated service, and wish her continued success in the future. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Julia Johnson. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 14, 2026 at 04:05 PM

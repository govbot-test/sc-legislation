A senate RESOLUTION

 

TO CONGRATULATE THE Fort Mill High School Girls Swimming Team, COACHES, AND SCHOOL OFFICIALS ON AN OUTSTANDING SEASON AND TO HONOR THEM FOR WINNING THE 5A Division II State Championship.

 

Whereas, the South Carolina Senate is proud to recognize the Fort Mill High School girls swimming team for winning the 5A Division II state championship; and

 

Whereas, this historic win marks the program's thirty-sixth state championship in school history, their third straight championship win, and the eighth title for the Fort Mill swim program; and

 

Whereas, in the championship game, Fort Mill won with a score of 483 points, and the team had several high finishes. Lila Hadley was third in the 200-yard individual medley in 2:08. Bella Corbi was fourth in 2:09 in the same event and had four top five finishes in the state meet; and

 

Whereas, the Fort Mill High School girls swim team team has flourished under the leadership and guidance of Coach Cindy Van Buskirk; and

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate appreciate the pride and recognition that the Fort Mill High School girls swim team has brought to its school and community and look forward to a future filled with further accomplishments by the team and its talented athletes.  Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the Senate:

 

That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, congratulate the Fort Mill High School girls swim team, coaches, and school officials on an outstanding season and honor them for winning the 5A Division II state championship.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the Fort Mill High School girls swim team, Coach Cindy Van Buskirk, and Principal Zachary D. Beam.

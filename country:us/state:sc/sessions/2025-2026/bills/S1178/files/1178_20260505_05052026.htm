A senate RESOLUTION

 

TO RECOGNIZE AND HONOR WARDEN JONATHAN NANCE FOR HIS ACT OF HEROISM AND to COMMEND HIS DEDICATed SERVICE TO the people and the state of south CAROLINA.

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate are pleased to recognize Warden Jonathan Nance for his selfless service to the people and the State of South Carolina; and

 

Whereas, on November 12, 2025, a distressed driver in a damaged vehicle flagged down Warden Nance while he was en route to his job at the Department of Corrections. Warden Nance recognized the driver was in a crisis and directed him to the prison parking lot where the driver unexpectedly brandished a revolver after parking his vehicle; and

 

Whereas, using steady verbal commands, clear communication, and coordination with responding staff, Warden Nance successfully de-escalated the situation, leading to the safe surrender of the weapon; and

 

Whereas, Warden Nance's sound decision making directly prevented injury or loss of life and created the conditions necessary for mental health intervention and emergency response personnel to arrive safely; and

 

Whereas, throughout the incident, Warden Nance continued to lead with compassion, ensuring the individual received medical attention, emotional support, and contact with his family; and

 

Whereas, Warden Nance's actions ultimately protected staff, the public, and the man in distress who was experiencing a severe mental health crisis following his involvement in multiple violent incidents earlier that morning; and

 

Whereas, this man's mother expressed gratitude for the professionalism and humanity Warden Nance demonstrated towards her son, emphasizing that his intervention prevented a tragic outcome; and

 

Whereas, Warden Nance's ability to recognize signs of crisis while simultaneously maintaining control of a volatile roadside situation reflects his unwavering professionalism and dedication to his community. His conduct exemplifies the highest standards of service and is a testament to his outstanding character. He is an invaluable asset to the Department of Corrections; and

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate greatly appreciate the dedication and commitment that Warden Nance has shown in serving the people and the State of South Carolina.  Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the Senate:

 

That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, recognize and honor Warden Jonathan Nance for his act of heroism and commend his dedicated service to the people and the State of South Carolina.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Warden Jonathan Nance.

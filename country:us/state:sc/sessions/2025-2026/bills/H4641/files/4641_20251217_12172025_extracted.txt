A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY AMENDING SECTION 44-53-190, RELATING TO SCHEDULE I CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES, SO AS TO ADD KRATOM TO THE CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES LISTED IN SCHEDULE I; AND BY REPEALING ARTICLE 20 OF CHAPTER 53, TITLE 44 RELATING TO THE SOUTH CAROLINA KRATOM CONSUMER PROTECTION ACT. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 44-53-190 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: (G) Kratom and any salt, sulfate, isomer, homologue, analogue, or other preparation of kratom, and any salt, isomer, compound, derivative, precursor, homologue, analogue, or other preparation thereof, synthetic or otherwise, that is substantially chemically equivalent or identical to kratom. SECTION 2.  Article 20, Chapter 53, Title 44 of the S.C. Code is repealed. SECTION 3.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on December 17, 2025 at 01:11 PM

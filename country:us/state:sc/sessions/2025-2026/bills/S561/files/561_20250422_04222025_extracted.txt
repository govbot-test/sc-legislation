2025-2026 Bill 561 Text of Previous Version (Apr. 22, 2025) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) Indicates Matter Stricken Indicates New Matter Recalled April 16, 2025 S. 561 Introduced by Senator Reichenbach S. Printed 4/16/25--S.   [SEC 4/22/2025 11:35 AM] Read the first time April 15, 2025 ________ A concurrent RESOLUTION TO REQUEST THAT THE DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION NAME the portion of River Road (state road S-40) from the intersection of Pine Street (S-21-312) to the intersection of Shirley Road (S-21-1114) IN Florence COUNTY "Billy Eaddy Road" AND ERECT APPROPRIATE MARKERS OR SIGNS AT THIS LOCATION CONTAINING THE DESIGNATION. Whereas, Mr. Lucious "Billy" Eaddy faithfully served the town of Pamplico as a town council member from 1976 until his passing in 2020. He was one of the two first Black officials for the town of Pamplico; and Whereas, Mr. Eaddy's family business was located on River Road in Pamplico, and many local citizens refer to this stretch of road as Billy Eaddy Road; and Whereas, it would be only fitting and proper to pay tribute to this son of South Carolina by naming a portion of road in the State in his honor. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring: That the members of the South Carolina General Assembly, by this resolution, request that the Department of Transportation name the portion of River Road (State Road S-40) from the intersection of Pine Street (S-21-312) to the intersection of Shirley Road (S-21-1114) in Florence County "Billy Eaddy Road" and erect appropriate markers or signs at this location containing the designation. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be forwarded to the Department of Transportation and presented to the family of Lucious "Billy" Eaddy. ----XX--- This web page was last updated on April 22, 2025 at 11:36 AM

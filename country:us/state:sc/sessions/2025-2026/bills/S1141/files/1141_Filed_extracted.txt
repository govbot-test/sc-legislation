Whereas, the Associated Builders and Contractors of the Carolinas is the voice of the merit shop construction industry, representing contractors, subcontractors, suppliers, and construction professionals across South Carolina and North Carolina and promoting the principles of free enterprise, fair competition, and opportunity for all; and Whereas, Associated Builders and Contractors of the Carolinas is committed to advancing workforce development, providing industry-leading safety and craft-training programs, and equipping construction professionals with the skills needed to succeed in a modern and growing economy; and Whereas, the organization plays a critical role in legislative advocacy and industry advancement, working to ensure that construction projects are awarded based on merit, safety, and value while strengthening the business climate and supporting thriving communities throughout the Carolinas; and Whereas, with a strong network of member companies and regional leadership councils across the Carolinas, Associated Builders and Contractors of the Carolinas fosters collaboration, professional development, and economic growth within the construction industry; and Whereas, the construction industry plays a vital role in South Carolina's economy and workforce, providing thousands of jobs and building the infrastructure that supports the state's continued growth; and Whereas, members of the Associated Builders and Contractors of the Carolinas will gather at the State House on April 22, 2026, to meet with members of the General Assembly and discuss issues impacting the construction industry and skilled trades workforce. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the members of the Senate: That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, recognize the Associated Builders and Contractors of the Carolinas for its contributions to the construction industry and workforce development and declare April 22, 2026, as "ABC Carolinas Hard Hat Day" at the State House. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the Associated Builders and Contractors of the Carolinas. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on April 22, 2026 at 7:56 PM

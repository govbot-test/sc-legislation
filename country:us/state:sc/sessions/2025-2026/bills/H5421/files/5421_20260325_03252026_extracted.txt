A house RESOLUTION TO RECOGNIZE AND HONOR RANDY CARTEE OF PICKENS COUNTY FOR HIS EXTRAORDINARY FIFTY-FIVE YEARS OF DEDICATED SERVICE AS A FIREFIGHTER AND TO CELEBRATE HIS ENDURING LEGACY IN HIS COMMUNITY AND THE PALMETTO STATE. Whereas, the House of Representatives find it is altogether fitting and proper to pause in its deliberations to recognize and honor the brave men and women who serve as first responders, willingly placing themselves in harm's way to protect life and property and to make our communities safer for all. Randy Cartee of Pickens County stands as a distinguished example of such selfless service, having devoted an exceptional fifty-five years as a volunteer firefighter; and Whereas, beginning at the age of seventeen, Randy Cartee has faithfully answered every call with courage, humility, and steadfast resolve, demonstrating an unwavering sense of duty from his first alarm to the present day, consistently attending trainings, drills, and meetings, and responding to countless emergencies, often at great personal sacrifice, to ensure the safety of others; and Whereas, in the face of danger and uncertainty, he has stood shoulder to shoulder with his fellow firefighters, meeting the challenges of the profession with strength, resilience, and an unshakable spirit, earning the deep respect and admiration of all who have served alongside him. Beyond his service on the front lines, he has been a trusted mentor and a steady leader, generously sharing his knowledge and wisdom to train, encourage, and inspire generations of firefighters, leaving an indelible mark on those who have followed in his footsteps while serving the citizens of Pickens County; and Whereas, whether at the station, in training, or responding to emergencies, Randy Cartee has been a constant and reassuring presence, known for his dependability, leadership, kindness, generosity, and the camaraderie he fostered among his crew, qualities that have created lasting bonds and memories, and for him, the role of firefighter has never been merely a duty, but a true calling defined by a lifetime of selfless service, quiet sacrifice, and enduring commitment to protecting his community; and Whereas, as he reaches this significant milestone with his official anniversary observed September 7th, it is fitting that the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives express their profound gratitude for his many years of faithful service and honor the countless lives he has touched and inspired throughout his remarkable career. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor Randy Cartee of Pickens County for his extraordinary fifty-five years of dedicated service as a firefighter and to celebrate his enduring legacy in his community and the Palmetto State. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Randy Cartee. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 25, 2026 at 11:46 AM

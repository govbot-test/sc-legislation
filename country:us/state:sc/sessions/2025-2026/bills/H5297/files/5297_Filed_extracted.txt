Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives were pleased to hear that at the 2026 South Carolina Dance Association (SCDA) State Dance Team Championship, on February 21, 2026, in Rock Hill, Taylor Rivers of the River Bluff High School Gator Dance Team was named the High School Solo State Champion; and Whereas, Miss Rivers, a freshman on the varsity Gator dance team, competed against over thirty of the top soloists in South Carolina. She won with a remarkable score of 280, besting second place by 1.5 points. This win is a testament to her hard work and dedication. She has put in countless hours of practice to perfect her technique while working toward this state championship title. This outstanding accomplishment brings honor and distinction to her team, school, and community; and Whereas, the River Bluff Gator Dance Team had an impressive performance at the 2026 SCDA State Dance Team Championship. The team earned multiple top three finishes in the varsity and junior varsity divisions. The team also had all of their soloists place in the top ten dancers. This performance is even more impressive as it is only the program's second year competing. During last year's SCDA State Championship, the Gators had many top five group finishes and five soloists, including the Solo State Champion Kylie Vaughan, place in the top ten; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives value the pride and recognition that Taylor Rivers has brought to her team, school, and community. They look forward to hearing about her continued achievements in the days to come. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor Taylor Rivers of River Bluff High School upon being named the 2026 High School Solo State Champion at the 2026 South Carolina Dance Association State Dance Team Championship. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Taylor Rivers. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 3, 2026 at 1:06 PM

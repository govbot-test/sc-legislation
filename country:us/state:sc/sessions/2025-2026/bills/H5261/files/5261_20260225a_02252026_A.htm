A joint Resolution

 

To extend the deadline for receiving alcohol server training as provided for in chapter 3, title 61 to MAY 1, 2026.

 

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina:

 

SECTION 1.  The deadline to receive alcohol server training as provided for in Chapter 3, Title 61 is extended to May 1, 2026.

 

SECTION 2.  This joint resolution takes effect upon approval by the Governor.

(R113, H5261)

A JOINT RESOLUTION TO EXTEND THE DEADLINE FOR RECEIVING ALCOHOL SERVER TRAINING AS PROVIDED FOR IN CHAPTER 3, TITLE 61 TO MAY 1, 2026.

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina:

Extension of alcohol server training deadline

SECTION 1.    The deadline to receive alcohol server training as provided for in Chapter 3, Title 61 is extended to May 1, 2026.

Time effective

SECTION 2.    This joint resolution takes effect upon approval by the Governor.

Ratified the 5th day of March, 2026.

Approved the 9th day of March, 2026. -- T.

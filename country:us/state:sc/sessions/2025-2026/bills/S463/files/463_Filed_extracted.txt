(A124, R141, S463) AN ACT TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY ADDING SECTION 50-21-128 SO AS TO PROHIBIT SWIMMING WITHIN FIFTY FEET OF A PUBLIC BOAT LANDING OR RAMP MAINTAINED BY THE SOUTH CAROLINA PUBLIC SERVICE AUTHORITY IF APPROPRIATELY MARKED. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: Swimming near a public boat landing or ramp prohibited SECTION 1.    Article 1, Chapter 21, Title 50 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: Section 50-21-128.    It is unlawful for a person to swim within fifty feet of a public boat landing or ramp maintained by the South Carolina Public Service Authority, if the landing or ramp is clearly marked with signage prohibiting swimming. Time effective SECTION 2.    This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. Ratified the 14th day of May, 2026.

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Rates for electricity and natural gas shall not be increased for a period of one year. However, rates for electricity or natural gas may decrease during this period. SECTION 2.  This joint resolution takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 31, 2026 at 2:59 PM

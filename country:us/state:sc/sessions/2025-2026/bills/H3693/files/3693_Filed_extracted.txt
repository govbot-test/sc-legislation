Whereas, the House of Representatives is pleased to learn that Greenwood County's Cambridge Academy's volleyball team captured the title at the state tournament in West Columbia on October 21, 2024; and Whereas, in their third straight appearance at a state match, the Cambridge Lady Cougars successfully delighted their faithful fans, sweeping Wardlaw Academy to take their program's first state trophy. With a 10-0 regular season, Cambridge defeated three teams in the upper bracket before facing off against Wardlaw. After a close first set, Cambridge took the last two sets handily for the state title; and Whereas, the Cambridge volleyball roster included Izzy Southerland, Addison Leopard, Aubrey Louden, Sophie Wilson, Ainsley Vickers, Mia Storer, Yzabel Cowan, Zaylee Landmesser, Khloe Cheeks, Marely Alvarez, and Avari Mitchell; and Whereas, in a sport demanding strength and speed, Head Coach Chelsie Calvert and her skilled staff used their own training to hone a championship team and teach these athletes lifelong principles; and Whereas, the members of the House value the pride that the volleyballers have brought to their school and their community, and they look forward to following their future achievements. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, honor the Cambridge Academy volleyball team, coaches, and school officials for a stellar season and congratulate them on winning the 2024 South Carolina Independent School Association Class 1A State title. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Head of School, Brad Griggs, and to Coach Chelsie Calvert. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 15, 2025 at 3:59 PM

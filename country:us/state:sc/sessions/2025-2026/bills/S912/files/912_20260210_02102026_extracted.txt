2025-2026 Bill 912 Text of Previous Version (Feb. 10, 2026) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Bill 912Indicates Matter StrickenIndicates New Matter(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) A senate RESOLUTION TO RECOGNIZE AND HONOR the Corey Mitchell Multiple Sclerosis Foundation. Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate are pleased to recognize the Corey Mitchell Multiple Sclerosis Foundation for supporting people who suffer from multiple sclerosis; and Whereas, Corey Mitchell, the founder and CEO of the Corey Mitchell Multiple Sclerosis Foundation, created the foundation to raise awareness about the disease and support people who have been diagnosed with multiple sclerosis, a chronic and often unpredictable disease that affects the central nervous system; and Whereas, the foundation's mission is to support individuals and families affected by multiple sclerosis through education, outreach, advocacy, and opportunity, while restoring hope and dignity to those who often feel unseen; and Whereas, the foundation has grown into a trusted community partner across the Midlands and beyond. The foundation hosts annual Health Fairs and Walks that provide education, resources, and access to care; organizes back-to-school initiatives that equip hundreds of children with supplies, ensuring no child starts the school year behind; and through the Helping Hands Scholarship Program, supports rising college students-particularly those entering the medical field-with the hope that one day they may help create better treatments, improved quality of life, or even a cure for multiple sclerosis; and Whereas, the foundation has been honored by partnerships with local governments, schools, law enforcement, civic organizations, and healthcare advocates. The foundation assures that families are supported and that the next generation is empowered to do better than the last; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate greatly appreciate the dedication and commitment that the Corey Mitchell Multiple Sclerosis Foundation has shown in serving the people and the State of South Carolina.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate: That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, recognize and honor the Corey Mitchell Multiple Sclerosis Foundation. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the founder and CEO of the Corey Mitchell Multiple Sclerosis Foundation, Corey Mitchell. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 10, 2026 at 02:32 PM

A house RESOLUTION To recognize and honor Dr. Dinesh Shukla, Chancellor and Founding President of the American International University West Africa, for transforming African Education Through Visionary Leadership. Whereas, born in Africa to Indian heritage and now a United States citizen, Dr. Shukla embodies a rare combination of global perspective and a deep commitment to Africa's development; and Whereas, he left home at thirteen to pursue education abroad, this early experience installed a great sense of independence, discipline, and intellectual resilience. He completed his secondary education at Kendriya Vidyalaya IIT Powai and his undergraduate studies at the University of Mumbai. He did graduate research at Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda, laying a strong foundation for a lifetime of dedication to education, healthcare, and service; and Whereas, Dr. Shukla first distinguished himself in healthcare leadership. While in Houston, Texas, he acquired and revitalized Northside General Hospital, where he turned a bankrupt 100-bed facility into a financially stable institution serving undeserved communities. His leadership extended to Guinea, where he chaired the American Hospital, the International Medical Center, improving access to quality medical care and public health outcomes; and Whereas, Dr. Shukla founded the American International University West Africa through a Presidential Decree in The Gambia in 2007. They opened in 2010 with six students. Since then, AIUWA has grown to over 2,000 students, offering American-standard medical and professional education aligned with regional health priorities, including collaboration with the West African Health Organization. He expanded his vision in 2023 to Sierra Leone, establishing a second university of 5,000 students, with the long-term goal of five universities across Africa that will educate 100,00 students by 2028; and Whereas, Dr. Shukla's leadership is defined by resilience, equity, and impact. Even during a serious cancer diagnosis in 2019, he remained actively engaged in advancing his institutions. By prioritizing access, scholarships, and ethical education, he has created a transformative model that equips graduates to serve society. Through his visionary leadership, institutional excellence, and continental expansion, Dr. Shukla continues to shape Africa's educational and healthcare landscape, empowering generations to contribute meaningfully to the continent's future. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor Dr. Dinesh Shukla, Chancellor and Founding President of the American International University West Africa, for transforming African education through visionary leadership. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Dr. Dinesh Shukla. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 24, 2026 at 12:54 PM

Newton and Herbkersman S. Printed 3/3/26--H. Read the first time February 4, 2026 ________ The BEAUFORT DELEGATION To whom was referred a Bill (H. 5089) to amend the South Carolina Code of Laws by amending Section 7-7-110, relating to designation of voting precincts in Beaufort County, so as to redesignate the map, etc., respectfully Report: That they have duly and carefully considered the same, and recommend that the same do pass: WILLIAM HERBKERSMAN for the Delegation. _______ A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY AMENDING SECTION 7-7-110, RELATING TO DESIGNATION OF VOTING PRECINCTS IN BEAUFORT COUNTY, SO AS TO REDESIGNATE THE MAP NUMBER ON WHICH THESE PRECINCTS ARE DELINEATED. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 7-7-110(B) of the S.C. Code is amended to read: (B) The precinct lines defining the above precincts are as shown on the official map prepared by and on file with the Revenue and Fiscal Affairs Office designated as document P-13-25P-13-26, and as shown on copies provided to the Board of Voter Registration and Elections of Beaufort County by the Revenue and Fiscal Affairs Office. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 03, 2026 at 02:04 PM

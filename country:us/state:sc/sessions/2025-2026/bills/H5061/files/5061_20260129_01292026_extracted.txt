2025-2026 Bill 5061 Text of Previous Version (Jan. 29, 2026) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Bill 5061Indicates Matter StrickenIndicates New Matter(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY ADDING SECTION 51-3-170 SO AS TO PROVIDE THAT A COASTAL BEACH IS A STATE PARK. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Chapter 3, Title 51 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: Section 51-3-170.  For purposes of the South Carolina Code of Laws, a coastal beach is considered a state park. For purposes of this section, a "coastal beach" means the area from the high-water mark to the ocean. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 29, 2026 at 10:52 AM

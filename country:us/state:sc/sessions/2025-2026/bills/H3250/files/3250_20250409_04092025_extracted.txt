Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) Committee Report April 9, 2025 H. 3250 Introduced by Rep. McGinnis S. Printed 4/9/25--H. Read the first time January 14, 2025 ________ The committee on House Education and Public Works To whom was referred a Bill (H. 3250) to amend the South Carolina Code of Laws by repealing Section 59-104-250 relating to the requirement that technical college libraries convert to computer-based automated, etc., respectfully Report: That they have duly and carefully considered the same, and recommend that the same do pass: SHANNON ERICKSON for Committee. _______ A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY REPEALING SECTION 59-104-250 RELATING TO THE REQUIREMENT THAT TECHNICAL COLLEGE LIBRARIES CONVERT TO COMPUTER-BASED AUTOMATED SYSTEMs COMPATIBLE WITH STATE LIBRARY SYSTEMS. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 59-104-250 of the S.C. Code is repealed. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on April 09, 2025 at 09:21 PM

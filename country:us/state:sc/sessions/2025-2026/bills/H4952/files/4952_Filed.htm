A house RESOLUTION

 

TO PROVIDE THAT THE StAFF SERVING THE MEMBERS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES IS NOT REQUIRED TO WORK ON GOOD FRIDAY, APRIL 3, 2026, AND EASTER MONDAY, APRIL 6, 2026.

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:

 

That the staff serving the members of the House of Representatives is not required to work on Good Friday, April 3, 2026, and Easter Monday, April 6, 2026.

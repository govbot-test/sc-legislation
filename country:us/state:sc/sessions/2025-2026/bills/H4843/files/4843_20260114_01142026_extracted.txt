A house RESOLUTION to recognize and honor Arrington Weston, a social studies teacher at Schofield Middle School in Aiken, and to commend His contributions to educational excellence. Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is grateful for outstanding teachers, like Arrington Weston, who give their time and talents to educate and prepare young people in our State to become productive citizens; and Whereas, Mr. Weston is a Golden Apple Award-winning educator and a Teacher of the Year, and he has been formally recognized by the South Carolina Legislative Black Caucus for providing excellent education and for uplifting his community; and Whereas, he gives credit to God for the grace to use every opportunity he finds to make a difference in the lives of young people. His work is a shining example of what is possible when anyone walks boldly in such a calling; and Whereas, Mr. Weston has founded transformative programs that meet the needs of the whole child. His literacy initiative has distributed close to five thousand books to students, which has opened doors to imagination, empowerment, and academic growth; and Whereas, his free haircut program has provided nearly a hundred students with services that restore dignity, build confidence, and remind children that they belong and have value. Through L.E.A.D. Club, an organization he guides with passion and purpose, students have completed over thirty hours of service to their school community, service which has taught them the values of responsibility, leadership, and giving back; and Whereas, a man of integrity, humility, and extraordinary dedication, Mr. Weston is a champion to his students and an asset to the great State of South Carolina. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor Arrington Weston, a social studies teacher at Schofield Middle School in Aiken, and commend his contributions to educational excellence. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Arrington Weston. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 14, 2026 at 02:59 PM

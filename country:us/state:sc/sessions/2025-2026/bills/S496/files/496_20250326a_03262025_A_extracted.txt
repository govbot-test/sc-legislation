2025-2026 Bill 496 Text of Previous Version (Mar. 26, 2025) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) Introduced March 26, 2025 S. 496 Introduced by Senate Labor, Commerce and Industry Committee S. Printed 3/26/25--S. Read the first time March 26, 2025 ________ A JOINT RESOLUTION TO APPROVE REGULATIONS OF THE Department of Labor, Licensing and Regulation - South Carolina Real Estate Appraisers Board, RELATING TO South Carolina Real Estate Appraisers Board, DESIGNATED AS REGULATION DOCUMENT NUMBER 5340, PURSUANT TO THE PROVISIONS OF ARTICLE 1, CHAPTER 23, TITLE 1 OF THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.   The regulations of the Department of Labor, Licensing and Regulation - South Carolina Real Estate Appraisers Board, relating to South Carolina Real Estate Appraisers Board, designated as Regulation Document Number 5340, and submitted to the General Assembly pursuant to the provisions of Article 1, Chapter 23, Title 1 of the S.C. Code, are approved. SECTION 2.   This joint resolution takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XXX---- SUMMARY AS SUBMITTED BY PROMULGATING AGENCY. The South Carolina Real Estate Appraisers Board proposes to amend Chapter 137 to conform to H.3278, passed in 2024 and to make various other changes. The Notice of Drafting was published in the State Register on May 24, 2024. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 26, 2025 at 08:47 PM

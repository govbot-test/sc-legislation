A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY AMENDING SECTION 12-36-2120, RELATING TO EXEMPTIONS FROM SALES TAX, SO AS TO PROVIDE FOR A SALES TAX EXEMPTION FOR BREAST PUMPS. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 12-36-2120 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: (85) breast pumps, breast pump collection and storage supplies, and breast pump kits. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor and first applies after 2024. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on December 12, 2024 at 04:34 PM

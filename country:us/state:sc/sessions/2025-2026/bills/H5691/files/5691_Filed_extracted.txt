Whereas, the American Hospital Association and the South Carolina Hospital Association have designated May 10-16, 2026, as Hospital Week in South Carolina; and Whereas, the purpose of Hospital Week is to acknowledge the work of hospitals in supporting the health needs of communities every day; and Whereas, South Carolina's hospitals and health systems contribute over thirty-six billion dollars in total economic impact, support thousands of jobs, and generate thirteen billion dollars in labor income statewide; and Whereas, the impact of hospitals is possible because of the preparation, dedication, and compassion of the health professionals they employ; and Whereas, on day shift and night shift throughout the entire year, health professionals working in hospitals strive to meet the healthcare needs of all South Carolina citizens and visitors to our State; and Whereas, the health and well-being of our communities and our people will ensure a prosperous, productive, and thriving State of South Carolina. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize May 10-16, 2026, as "Hospital Week in South Carolina." ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 12, 2026 at 12:43 PM

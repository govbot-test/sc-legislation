A house RESOLUTION To celebrate the dorman high school girls golf team for winning the 2024 south Carolina high school league class AAAAA state championship title. Whereas, on October 30, 2024, the members of the Dorman High School girls golf team packed up a coveted trophy, the impressive proof of their first-place victory at the 2024 South Carolina High School League Class AAAAA State Championship tournament, held at Columbia Country Club in Columbia; and Whereas, the Lady Cavaliers entered the season as the back-to-back defending state champions.  Returning four golfers from the 2023 state championship team, the girls commenced the season looking to continue their reign atop Class AAAAA. On October 29-30, the Dorman Cavaliers competed at the South Carolina High School League Class AAAAA state golf championship; and Whereas, entering the tournament, Lexington, River Bluff, and Carolina Forest all felt they could contend for the team title. The Cavaliers began the tournament strong on day one, with junior Ava Romansky, opening with three birdies in a row. Following the first day of competition, the Cavaliers held an 11-stroke lead on Carolina Forest; and Whereas, new this year the tournament featured live scoring for golf. After each hole, the coaches and teams could see exactly where they stood. With this knowledge, any attempt at a comeback slammed shut when the Lady Cavaliers posted a strong front nine on day two. By the time they made the turn, the Cavaliers knew they didn't have to take any big chances. They just needed to get safely to the clubhouse. Led by Londyn Rath, Ava Romansky, and AC Peake, the Lady Cavaliers earned a 12-stroke victory and won the 2024 South Carolina High School League Class AAAAA state golf championship; and Whereas, in a sport that demands the patience and skill, Head Coach Laura Maurer and her outstanding assistant coaches, Kristen Duncan, Arista Edwards, and Arran Setro, used their own athletic ability and training to build a championship team, which included Addison Cox, Marlee Metcalf, AC Peake, Londyn Rath, and Ava Romansky. The coaches taught these athletes lessons that will prove invaluable throughout life, both on and off the fairways and greens; and Whereas, the House of Representatives takes great pleasure in applauding the Lady Cavaliers on a fine season, and the members look forward to hearing of their further accomplishments in the days ahead.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, celebrate the Dorman High School girls golf team on winning the 2024 South Carolina High School League Class AAAAA State Championship title. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Principal Bryant Roberson and Head Coach Laura Maurer of Dorman High School. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 25, 2025 at 01:31 PM

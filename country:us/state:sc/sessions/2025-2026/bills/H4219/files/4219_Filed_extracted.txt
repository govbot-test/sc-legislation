Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is pleased to learn that the members of the Chesnee High School varsity girls golf team captured the top prize at their state tournament, held at Chester Country Club, October 28-29, 2024; and Whereas, the Lady Eagles' dominant 2024 performance earned them a fourth consecutive state title, a feat that may force them to enlarge their present display case for the better accommodation of their ever-increasing collection of hardware; and Whereas, having accumulated a 53-shot lead after the first of two rounds, the Lady Eagles trounced runner-up Atlantic Collegiate Academy by 119 strokes, giving Chesnee an impressive final team score of 581; and Whereas, Chesnee senior Olivia Roberts won medalist honors by four strokes with a 10 under par 134. Senior Olivia Shields was third with a 148, while senior Addison Parker was fifth with a 150. Juniors Jada Flynn (160) and Abigail Watson (161) finished seventh and eighth, respectively. Rounding out the team roster was junior Abby Herrington; and Whereas, with justifiable pride, Head Coach Kyle Scruggs and his fine staff watched the Lady Eagles raise their brand-new state title trophy; and Whereas, appreciative of the deservedly honored reputation of the Chesnee High varsity girls golf team and coaches, the House is delighted to add a full meed of praise to the voices of the many admirers of these fine athletes on the occasion of the acquisition of their newest state title. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and celebrate the Chesnee High School varsity girls golf team, coaches, and school officials for an extraordinary season and congratulate them on winning the 2024 South Carolina High School League Class AA State Championship title. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Principal Erik Gerstenacker and Head Coach Kyle Scruggs of Chesnee High School. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 25, 2025 at 1:52 PM

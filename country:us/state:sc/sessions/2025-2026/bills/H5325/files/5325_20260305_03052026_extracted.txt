A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY ADDING ARTICLE 29 TO CHAPTER 1, TITLE 1 SO AS TO PROVIDE THAT THE GOD OF ABRAHAM, ISAAC, AND JACOB IS THE ONLY TRUE CREATOR GOD RECOGNIZED BY THIS STATE. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Chapter 1, Title 1 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: Article 29 God of Abraham, Issac, and Jacob Section 1-1-1810.  The State of South Carolina affirms that the God of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob is the only true Creator God recognized by this State or political subdivision thereof, including school districts. This recognition is based on the historical and foundational principles referenced in the Declaration of Independence, United States Constitution, and Constitution of this State. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 05, 2026 at 12:04 PM

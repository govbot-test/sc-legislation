A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY ADDING SECTION 53-3-320 SO AS TO DECLARE THE TWENTY-THIRD DAY OF APRIL OF EACH YEAR AS "TABLE TENNIS DAY" IN SOUTH CAROLINA. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Chapter 3, Title 53 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: Section 53-3-320.  The twenty-third day of April of each year is designated as "Table Tennis Day" in South Carolina. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 29, 2025 at 03:00 PM

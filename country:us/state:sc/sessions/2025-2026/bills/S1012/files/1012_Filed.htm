A concurrent RESOLUTION

 

TO invite the Chief Justice of the South Carolina Supreme Court, the Honorable John W. Kittredge, to address the General Assembly in Joint Session on the State of the Judiciary in the Hall of the House of Representatives at 12:00 noon on Wednesday, April 15, 2026.

 

Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:

 

That the members of the South Carolina General Assembly, by this resolution, invite the Chief Justice of the South Carolina Supreme Court, the Honorable John W. Kittredge, to address the General Assembly in Joint Session on the State of the Judiciary in the Hall of the House of Representatives at 12:00 noon on Wednesday, April 15, 2026.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Chief Justice Kittredge.

A house RESOLUTION

 

to recognize and honor Main Street methodist church of abbeville on the occasion of its two hundredth anniversary and to commend the church for two centuries of faithful service to god and the community. 

 

Whereas, for two hundred years, Main Street Methodist Church has stood as a steadfast and welcoming presence in the Abbeville Community; and

 

Whereas, the congregation traces its origins to 1825, when Abbeville's first Methodist congregation was established on Washington Street under the leadership of a young Englishwoman, Ann Fisher Moore, whose personal conversion inspired a legacy of faith that continues to this day; and

 

Whereas, in 1840, the congregation relocated to its present site on North Main Street, where it continued to flourish. The current sanctuary was dedicated in 1888, with stained-glass windows and a bell tower added in 1890, and further renovations completed in 1895 to accommodate a growing membership following the arrival of the railroad; and

 

Whereas, throughout the twentieth and twenty-first centuries, Main Street Methodist Church has expanded both its facilities and its ministry, including the addition of the Education Building in 1925, the donation of the Greene Center in 1993, and the dedication of a restored, custom-built 649-pipe organ in 2019, reflecting its enduring commitment to worship through music; and

 

Whereas, today, Main Street Methodist Church remains a Christ-centered, family oriented, and mission-driven congregation, devoted to glorifying God through worship, discipleship, fellowship, and service within the community and beyond; and

 

Whereas, for two centuries, Main Street Methodist Church has served as a beacon of faith and a cornerstone of the Abbeville community, and, by God's grace, will continue its legacy for generations to come. Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:

 

That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor Main Street Methodist Church of Abbeville on the occasion of its two hundredth anniversary and commend the church for two centuries of faithful service to God and the community.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Main Street Methodist Church.

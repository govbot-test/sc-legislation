Read the first time March 25, 2026 ________ A bill TO PROVIDE THAT SINGLE-FAMILY DWELLING UNITS IN THE BLUE RIDGE COMMUNITY IN GREENVILLE COUNTY MUST BE BUILT ON FIVE ACRE TRACTS, TO PROVIDE THAT MULTI-FAMILY DWELLINGS BUILT IN THE BLUE RIDGE COMMUNITY MUST BE BUILT ON A TRACT OF LAND THAT IS EQUAL TO FIVE ACRES OF LAND FOR EACH DWELLING UNIT, AND TO PROVIDE FOR AN EXCEPTION. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  After the effective date of this act, any new single-family homes in the Blue Ridge Community, as established by Act 108 of 2021 and as amended by Act 14 of 2025, must be constructed on a tract of land comprised of not less than five acres. SECTION 2.  After the effective date of this act, any new multi-family dwellings in the Blue Ridge Community must be constructed on a tract of land comprised of not less than five acres for each dwelling unit in the multi-family dwelling. SECTION 3.  The provisions of this act do not apply to a person to whom a tract of land comprised of less than five acres is conveyed, bequeathed, or devised from a family member if the person maintains ownership of the tract for his use and enjoyment or the use and enjoyment of his immediate family. SECTION 4.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 25, 2026 at 10:18 PM

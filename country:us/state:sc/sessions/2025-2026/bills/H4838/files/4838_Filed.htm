A house RESOLUTION

 

TO CONGRATULATE Susana G. Hines OF Florence COUNTY ON THE OCCASION OF HER ONE HUNDREDTH BIRTHDAY AND TO WISH HER A JOYOUS BIRTHDAY CELEBRATION AND CONTINUED HEALTH AND HAPPINESS.

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives are delighted to learn that Susana G. Hines of Florence County will celebrate her one hundredth birthday on June 21, 2025; and

 

Whereas, born in Baltimore, Maryland, on June 21, 1925, in the first quarter of the twentieth century, she has witnessed the many remarkable social changes, technological advances, and international developments that have spanned the globe from that time into the first quarter of the twenty-first century; and

 

Whereas, the daughter of the late Samuel and Susana Gregg, young Susana eventually came to live with her grandmother in Florence; and

 

Whereas, together with her beloved husband, the late Mack T. Hines Sr., she reared five fine children: the late Reverend Dr. Mack T. Hines, a pillar of the Florence community, pastor of St. Paul Baptist Church, and South Carolina State Representative from 1995 to 2005; Susie Cannon; Betty McNeil; Samson Hines; and the late Bobbie Hough; and

 

Whereas, a devoted and loving mother and grandmother, Mrs. Hines is known for her wisdom, warmth, and hospitality. In fact, she always kept pitchers of her famous  lemonade and iced tea ready for anyone who needed a refreshing drink. Mrs. Hines is a devout Christian whose life reflects the values of hard work, compassion, and dedication to family and friends; and

 

Whereas, over the years, she has enjoyed gardening, cooking, and a good game of checkers. She is a member of Salem United Methodist Church where she proudly holds the distinction and honor of being the church's eldest member; and

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives are pleased to honor this daughter of South Carolina at the celebration of her one hundredth birthday and join with her family and friends in congratulating her on reaching this extraordinary milestone. Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:

 

That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, congratulate Susana G. Hines of Florence County on the occasion of her one hundredth birthday and wish her a joyous birthday celebration and continued health and happiness.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Susana G. Hines.

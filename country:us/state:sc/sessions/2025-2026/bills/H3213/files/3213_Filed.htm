A joint Resolution

 

TO PLACE A MORATORIUM ON THE ENFORCEMENT OF ARCHITECTURAL REVIEW ORDINANCES FOR COMMERCIAL PROPERTIES UNTIL JULY 1, 2028.

 

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina:

 

SECTION 1.  Local ordinances or regulations for architectural review of commercial properties in this State may not be enforced until July 1, 2028. The provisions of this joint resolution:

    (1) apply notwithstanding the provisions of Article 5, Chapter 29, Title 6, or another provision of law; and

    (2) do not apply to single-family residences.

 

SECTION 2.  This joint resolution takes effect on July 1, 2026.

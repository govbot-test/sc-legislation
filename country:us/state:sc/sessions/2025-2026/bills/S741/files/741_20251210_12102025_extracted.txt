A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY ADDING SECTION 44-37-55 SO AS TO PROVIDE THAT NO VACCINE CAN BE MANDATED FOR INFANTS UNDER THE AGE OF TWENTY-FOUR MONTHS. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Chapter 37, Title 44 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: Section 44-37-55.  No vaccine can be mandated for infants under the age of twenty-four months. An infant under the age of twenty-four months may receive a vaccine at the voluntary request of the infant's parents. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on December 10, 2025 at 02:34 PM

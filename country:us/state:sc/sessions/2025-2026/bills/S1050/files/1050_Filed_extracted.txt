Read the first time March 25, 2026 ________ A bill TO PROVIDE THAT EACH MEMBER OF THE HAMPTON COUNTY TRANSPORTATION COMMITTEE SHALL RECEIVE ONE HUNDRED DOLLARS FOR EACH MEETING AT WHICH HE IS IN ATTENDANCE AND THAT THE CHAIRMAN SHALL RECEIVE ONE HUNDRED SEVENTY-FIVE DOLLARS, AND TO PROVIDE THAT THE CHAIRMAN SHALL NOT APPROVE SUCH PAYMENTS MORE THAN EIGHTEEN TIMES EACH FISCAL YEAR. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  SECTION 1. Notwithstanding another provision of law, each member of the Hampton County Transportation Committee, in performance of his duties, shall be allowed and paid from Hampton County "C" fund revenues the sum of one hundred dollars for each meeting at which he is in attendance. The chairman of the committee shall be allowed and paid from the Hampton County "C" fund revenue the sum of one hundred seventy-five dollars for each meeting at which he is in attendance. SECTION 2. The Hampton County Transportation Committee shall receive the payment authorized herein upon issuance of approved vouchers by its chairman; provided, however, the chairman may not approve vouchers in any single fiscal year which vouchers authorize payment for more than eighteen meetings per fiscal year for each member of the committee. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 25, 2026 at 10:20 PM

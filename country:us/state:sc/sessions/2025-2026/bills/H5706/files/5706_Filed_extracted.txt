MANN "BUBBA" CROMER. Whereas, William Hewitt "Witt" Mitchell has served with distinction as a page for the South Carolina House of Representatives since 2024 and was selected to serve as acting reading clerk in the absence of Mr. James L. Mann "Bubba" Cromer; and Whereas, through his dedication, professionalism, and enthusiasm for the institution of South Carolina state government, Witt has demonstrated exemplary character, leadership, and commitment to public service; and Whereas, his strong work ethic, respect for the legislative process, and willingness to assume additional responsibilities reflect great credit upon himself, his family, and the South Carolina House of Representatives; and Whereas, the members of the House of Representatives desire to publicly recognize and commend Witt for his outstanding contributions and faithful service to the people of this State. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor William Hewitt "Witt" Mitchell for his exemplary service as a page to the South Carolina House of Representatives since 2024 and commend him for his willingness to serve as acting reading clerk in the absence of the distinguished James L. Mann "Bubba" Cromer. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to William Hewitt "Witt" Mitchell. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 14, 2026 at 3:39 PM

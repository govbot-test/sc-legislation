2025-2026 Bill 1112 Text of Previous Version (Apr. 15, 2026) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Bill 1112Indicates Matter StrickenIndicates New Matter(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) A senate RESOLUTION TO RECOGNIZE May 10-16, 2026, AS "SOuth Carolina Hospital Week." Whereas, the American Hospital Association and the South Carolina Hospital Association have designated May 10-16, 2026, as Hospital Week in South Carolina; and Whereas, the purpose of Hospital Week is to acknowledge the work of hospitals in supporting the health needs of communities every day; and Whereas, South Carolina's hospitals and health systems contribute over thirty-six billion dollars in total economic impact, support more than two hundred three thousand jobs, and generate thirteen billion dollars in labor income statewide; and Whereas, the impact of hospitals is possible because of the preparation, dedication, and compassion of the health professionals they employ; and Whereas, health professionals working in hospitals strive to meet the healthcare needs of all South Carolina citizens and guests to our State, on day shift and night shift throughout the entire year; and Whereas, the health and wellbeing of our communities and our people will ensure a prosperous, productive, and thriving State of South Carolina.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate: That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, recognize May 10-16, 2026, as "South Carolina Hospital Week." ----XX---- This web page was last updated on April 15, 2026 at 02:51 PM

Smith, Spann-Wilder, Stavrinakis, Taylor, Teeple, Terribile, Vaughan, Weeks, Wetmore, Wheeler, White, Whitmire, Wickensimer, Williams, Willis, Wooten and YowDocument Path: LC-0233WAB-GM25.docxIntroduced in the House on January 15, 2025Adopted by the House on January 15, 2025Summary: Latta High School FFA champsHISTORY OF LEGISLATIVE ACTIONS Date Body Action Description with journal page number 1/15/2025 House Introduced and adopted (House Journal-page 17) View the latest legislative information at the websiteVERSIONS OF THIS BILL01/15/2025 A house RESOLUTION to recognize and honor the Latta High School Future Farmers of America team, sponsor aaron miles, and school officials and to congratulate them for winning the State Farm Business Management competition. Whereas, the House of Representatives is pleased to learn that the Latta High School Future Farmers of America (FFA) team captured the State Farm Business Management competition; and Whereas, the five students on the Latta team, Kate Gasque, Brooke Gasque, Layce Arnette, Hendley Ann McIntyre, and Lydia George, traveled to Greenville for the annual State FFA Convention where they competed in the event and other activities; and Whereas, Lydia George had the second highest score in the State and all four scores were in the top twelve scores in the State. They advanced to the national competition at the Annual FFA Convention in October in Indianapolis, Indiana; and Whereas, Latta High School FFA has a long history of outstanding success, and these five young ladies have continued that tradition. FFA Sponsor Aaron Miles challenges his students to achieve higher and higher goals during their tenure in his agricultural program, and the results have been impressive; and Whereas, the House of Representatives values the dedication of the Latta FFA team and the pride to their school, and the members look forward to hearing of the team's continued success. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor the Latta High School Future Farmers of America team, Sponsor Aaron Miles, and school officials and congratulate them for winning the State Farm Business Management competition. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Latta FFA Sponsor Aaron Miles. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 15, 2025 at 3:52 PM

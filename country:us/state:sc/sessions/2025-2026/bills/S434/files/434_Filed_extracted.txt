2025-2026 Bill 434: Wrestler Cason Howle - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Download This Bill in Microsoft Word FormatIndicates Matter StrickenIndicates New MatterS. 434STATUS INFORMATIONSenate ResolutionSponsors: Senator GarrettDocument Path: LC-0189HDB-GM25.docxIntroduced in the Senate on March 11, 2025Adopted by the Senate on March 11, 2025Summary: Wrestler Cason HowleHISTORY OF LEGISLATIVE ACTIONS Date Body Action Description with journal page number 3/11/2025 Senate Introduced and adopted (Senate Journal-page 8) View the latest legislative information at the websiteVERSIONS OF THIS BILL03/11/2025 A senate RESOLUTION To recognize and honor cason Howle, a senior wrestler for Greenwood High School, for winning the 2025 South Carolina State Championship title and to congratulate him for a spectacular high school wrestling career. Whereas, by his senior year in the 2024-2025 season, Cason Howle was a three-time captain of the Greenwood High School wrestling team, helping to lead the team to twenty-two wins and another deep run in the playoffs. With his assistance, his team placed fourth in the individual state tournament out of forty-seven other class AAAAA teams; and Whereas, as an individual, he garnered forty-seven wins with no losses in the 2024-2025 season. He allowed not a single opponent to score on him all season, and he finished his high school career as a four-time state champion with two hundred sixteen wins and one loss; and Whereas, Cason was the three-time All Lakelands Champion, two-time Southern Slam Champion, Newberry Open Champion, three-time NCHSAA All-American, two-time World Team Trials All-American, and Fargo All-American; and Whereas, the South Carolina Wrestling Coaches Association named Cason the Wrestler of the Year for the 2024-2025 season in all classifications. The members of the South Carolina Senate wish him lasting success as he continues his wrestling career at North Carolina State University this upcoming fall. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate: That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, recognize and honor Cason Howle, a senior wrestler for Greenwood High School, for winning the 2025 South Carolina State Championship title and congratulate him for a spectacular high school wrestling career. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Cason Howle. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 11, 2025 at 12:34 PM

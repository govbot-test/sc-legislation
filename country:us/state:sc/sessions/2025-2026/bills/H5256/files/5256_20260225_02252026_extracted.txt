A house RESOLUTION to congratulate the Northwood academy archery team, coaches, and school officials on an outstanding season and to honor them for winning the 2026 south carolina independent school association (SCISA) state championship. Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is proud to recognize the Northwood Academy archery team for winning the 2026 South Carolina Independent School Association (SCISA) State Championship title for the seventh year in a row; and Whereas, on February 14, 2026, the Northwood Academy Chargers secured the title as part of their seventh consecutive win streak; and Whereas, the Northwood Academy archery team has flourished under the leadership and guidance of Head Coach Doug McLeod; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives appreciate the pride and recognition that the Northwood Academy archery team has brought to its school and community and look forward to a future filled with further accomplishments by the team and its talented athletes.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, congratulate the Northwood Academy archery team, coaches, and school officials on an outstanding season and honor them for winning the 2026 South Carolina Independent School Association (SCISA) State Championship. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Dr. Christina Furrow, Head of School, and Head Coach Doug McLeod. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 25, 2026 at 11:29 AM

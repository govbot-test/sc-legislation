A senate RESOLUTION

 

TO RECOGNIZE AND HONOR the members of the Phillips family upon the OCCASION of their family reunion.

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate are pleased to recognize the Phillips family upon the occasion of their family reunion on April 17, 2026; and

 

Whereas, the Phillips family, including the branches of the Phillips-Jeffcoat and Phillips-Smith families, has established a proud and lasting legacy in Columbia; and

 

Whereas, through generations of service, leadership, sacrifice, and excellence, members of the Phillips family have made meaningful contributions across several industries and professions that have helped shape, strengthen, and uplift the community; and

 

Whereas, members of the Phillips family have served our nation with honor and distinction as military veterans, demonstrating courage, patriotism, discipline, and an unwavering commitment to the ideals and freedoms we cherish; and

 

Whereas, as elected officials and dedicated public servants, members of this distinguished family have also helped shape their community through leadership and public service, helping to advance progress and represent the voices, interests, and aspirations of the communities they serve; and

 

Whereas, members of the Phillips family have made a lasting impact in the field of education through their inspiring teaching and mentorship, as well in civic leadership, serving as postmasters, government officials, technology professionals, and other trusted leaders in their community; and

 

Whereas, members of this esteemed family have also made notable contributions to sports and athletics, and the arts, demonstrating teamwork, discipline, perseverance, and community pride both on and off the field, while serving as role models for younger generations; and

 

Whereas, civic leaders, the members of the Phillips family have also distinguished themselves through nonprofit leadership, charitable service, and community outreach; and

 

Whereas, this family reunion serves not only as a joyful celebration of heritage, kinship, and shared history, but also as a meaningful reminder of the importance of family unity, generational legacy, and the enduring influence of those who came before us; and

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate greatly appreciate the dedication and commitment that the Phillips family has shown in serving the people and the State of South Carolina.  Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the Senate:

 

That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, recognize and honor the members of the Phillips family upon the occasion of their family reunion.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the Phillips family.

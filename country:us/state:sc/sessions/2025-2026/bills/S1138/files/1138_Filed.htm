Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate are proud to recognize Jada M. Samuel of Greenville for winning Miss South Carolina 2023 on June 24, 2023, and serving as a significant ambassador for the Palmetto State during her reign; and

 

Whereas, possessing both talent and intelligence, the twenty-six-year-old beauty competed as Miss Bridge City and was crowned Miss South Carolina in the finals of the competition held at the historic Township Auditorium in Columbia; and

 

Whereas, Miss Samuel is grateful for the opportunity her title is giving her to advocate as a creative communicator, change agent, and champion for girls. For her talent, she performed a monologue and won the Miss Evening Gown Award; and

 

Whereas, she has earned over eighty-five thousand dollars in scholarships through the Miss South Carolina Scholarship Organization for her placement in the competition, her community service, and dedication to her platform; and

 

Whereas, after graduating from the University of South Carolina, Miss Samuel began her career as a lifestyle reporter before transitioning into the world of politics as a digital strategist. She earned an MBA with a focus in marketing from Louisiana State University Shreveport in 2023 and currently works with SCETV as an on-air host and producer; and

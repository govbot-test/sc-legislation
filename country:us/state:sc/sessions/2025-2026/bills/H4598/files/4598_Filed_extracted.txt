Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 12-37-220(B) of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: (54) one personal motor vehicle owned or leased by an individual who has reached the age of sixty-five years on or before December thirty-first. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor and first applies to property tax years after 2025. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 13, 2026 at 2:42 PM

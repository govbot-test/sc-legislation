Printed 3/25/25--H. Read the first time January 30, 2025 ________ A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY ADDING SECTION 40-45-285 SO AS TO PROVIDE PHYSICAL THERAPISTS MAY CERTIFY THAT AN INDIVIDUAL IS HANDICAPPED AND DECLARE THAT THE HANDICAP IS TEMPORARY OR PERMANENT FOR PURPOSES OF THE INDIVIDUAL'S APPLICATION FOR A HANDICAPPED PLACARD. Amend Title To Conform Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Article 1, Chapter 45, Title 40 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: Section 40-45-285. A physical therapist may certify that an individual is handicapped and declare that the handicap is temporary for purposes of the individual's application for a handicapped placard. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 25, 2025 at 04:51 PM

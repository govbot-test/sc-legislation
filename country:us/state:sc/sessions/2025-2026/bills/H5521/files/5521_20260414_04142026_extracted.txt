2025-2026 Bill 5521 Text of Previous Version (Apr. 14, 2026) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Bill 5521Indicates Matter StrickenIndicates New Matter(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) A house RESOLUTION to express strong support for the Big Homie Lil Homie Mentoring Program and its mission to uplift and empower youth through meaningful mentorship; to encourage community members, local leaders, businesses, and organizations to partner with, volunteer for, and invest in the success of the program; and to encourage the exploration of opportunities to provide resources, visibility, and institutional support to expand the outreach and impact of the program. Whereas, Big Homie Lil Homie is a nonprofit organization that uplifts young males by molding and shaping them into great men of society through mentoring, and Whereas, the Big Homie Lil Homie mentoring program is designed to connect experienced, responsible mentors, Big Homies, with youth, Lil Homies, to foster personal growth, leadership development, accountability, and community engagement; and Whereas, the program promotes values including respect, integrity, education, conflict resolution, and civic responsibility; and Whereas, mentoring relationships have been shown to improve outcomes in education, to reduce involvement in negative behaviors, and to strengthen community ties; and Whereas, the Big Homie Lil Homie mentoring program seeks to create safe spaces for dialogue, skill building, and empowerment, particularly for male youth who may face systemic barriers or limited access to resources; and Whereas, supporting mentorship initiatives contributes to building stronger, safer, and more connected communities. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, express strong support for the Big Homie Lil Homie mentoring program and its mission to uplift and empower youth through meaningful mentorship; encourage community members, local leaders, businesses, and organizations to partner with, volunteer for, and invest in the success of the program; and encourage the exploration of opportunities to provide resources, visibility, and institutional support to expand the outreach and impact of the program. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the leadership of Big Homie Lil Homie. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on April 14, 2026 at 01:07 PM

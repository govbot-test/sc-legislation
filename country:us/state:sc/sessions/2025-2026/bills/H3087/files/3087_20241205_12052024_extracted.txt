A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY AMENDING SECTION 20-3-10, RELATING TO GROUNDS FOR DIVORCE, SO AS TO INCLUDE PHYSICAL CRUELTY TOWARD A MINOR CHILD OF THE PETITIONING PARTY AS A GROUND FOR DIVORCE. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 20-3-10(3) of the S.C. Code is amended to read: (3) physical cruelty toward the petitioning party or a minor child of the petitioning party; SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on December 06, 2024 at 10:37 AM

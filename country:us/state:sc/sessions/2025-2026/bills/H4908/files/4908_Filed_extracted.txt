Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives takes great pride in the accomplishments of Mathieu Curtis of Tega Cay, a distinguished graduate of Fort Mill High School's Class of 2022; and Whereas, Mathieu was a top pitcher for the Fort Mill High School baseball program, where he laid the foundation for his increasing athletic success, first at the collegiate level and now at the professional level; and Whereas, after continuing his development as a baseball player at the University of South Carolina Upstate in Spartanburg and then at Virginia Tech in Blacksburg, Virginia, Mathieu Curtis was selected in the fourteenth round of the 2025 Major League Baseball Draft by the Atlanta Braves, a remarkable milestone that reflects his skill, dedication, and perseverance; and Whereas, his success stands as a testament to years of hard work, as well as to the steadfast support of his coaches, teammates, and family, and serves as an inspiration to young athletes across the Fort Mill community; and Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is delighted to extend congratulations to Mathieu Curtis as he begins his professional baseball career, and the members look forward to following his continued success with much interest and anticipation. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor Mathieu Curtis of Tega Cay for his outstanding achievements in baseball, congratulate him on being selected in the 2025 Major League Baseball Draft, and commend the foundations of excellence he built at Fort Mill High School and in college. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Mathieu Curtis. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 14, 2026 at 4:18 PM

Whereas, celebrated every April in the United States, School Library Month is spearheaded by the American Association of School Librarians to highlight the vital role of school libraries and certified librarians in transforming learning, encouraging reading, supporting student success, and building strong school communities. Activities such as book swaps, author visits, and themed displays entertainingly invite library patrons to explore the library and learn more of its many services and offerings; and Whereas, school libraries provide equitable access to high-quality books, digital resources, and technology while promoting literacy, fostering a love of reading, and teaching critical research and information literacy skills; and Whereas, school librarians collaborate with teachers to support instruction; strengthen curriculum; and empower students to become curious, capable, and informed learners; and Whereas, School Library Month also highlights the library as a welcoming space where all students can explore ideas, develop creativity, and grow both academically and personally; and Whereas, the House of Representatives is pleased to encourage the citizens of our great State, most particularly its students, to join in celebrating School Library Month this April, and, moreover, the members encourage our young people to patronize and enjoy their school libraries throughout the school year. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, declare the month of April 2026 as "School Library Month" in the State of South Carolina. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 28, 2026 at 2:46 PM

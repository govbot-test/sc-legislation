A house RESOLUTION

 

TO CELEBRATE THE CHESNEE HIGH SCHOOL COMPETITIVE CHEER SQUAD, ITS COACHES, AND SCHOOL OFFICIALS FOR A STELLAR SEASON AND TO CONGRATULATE THEM ON WINNING THE 2025 CLASS AAA STATE CHAMPIONSHIP TITLE.

 

Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is pleased to learn that the members of the Chesnee High School competitive cheer squad took top honors at the state cheer competition, held at Colonial Life Arena in Columbia on Saturday, November 22, 2025; and

 

Whereas, to the delight of their exuberant fans, the Chesnee High Lady Eagles bested all comers to earn the state title with a score of 286.5 points, leaving behind their nearest opponent, Mid-Carolina, as a distant second; and

 

Whereas, further, with their back-to-back wins in 2024 and 2025, the Lady Eagles extended their championship total by making it the team's sixth state triumph overall. The Lady Eagles were runners-up for the state title in 2022 and 2023; and

 

Whereas, in a sport that requires precision, grace, and strength, Head Coach Allie Tyner and Assistant Coaches Jada Churchill and McKenzie Scruggs capitalized on their own experience and passion to shape a championship-caliber team. Together, they taught their athletes lessons that will prove invaluable throughout life, both on and off the mats; and

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives appreciate the pride and recognition the Chesnee cheerleaders have brought to their school and community and look to hear of their continued achievements in the days ahead. Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:

 

That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, celebrate the Chesnee High School competitive cheer squad, its coaches, and school officials for a stellar season and congratulate them on winning the 2025 Class AAA State Championship title.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Principal Steven Fusaro and Head Coach Allie Tyner of Chesnee High School.

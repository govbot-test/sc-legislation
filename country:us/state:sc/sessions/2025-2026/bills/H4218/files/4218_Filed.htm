A house RESOLUTION

 

TO RECOGNIZE AND HONOR THE Chesnee High School competitive cheer squad, COACHES, AND SCHOOL OFFICIALS FOR AN OUTSTANDING SEASON AND TO CONGRATULATE THEM FOR WINNING THE 2024 SOUTH CAROLINA CLASS AAA STATE CHAMPIONSHIP TITLE.

 

Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is pleased to learn that the members of the Chesnee High competitive cheer squad of Spartanburg County captured the top honors at the state competition held at the Colonial Life Arena in Columbia on Saturday, November 23, 2024; and

 

Whereas, to the delight of their faithful fans, the Chesnee Lady Eagles defeated a fine field of competitors with a score of 278 besting their nearest opponent, Palmetto High School by 16.5 points; and

 

Whereas, a field of judges decides the cheer competition on each squad's performance and execution of jumps, stunts, tumbling, and transitions and the degree of the program's difficulty and choreography. Making the win all the sweeter, Chesnee won the Class AAA championship as a smaller Class AA school; and

 

Whereas, twenty-six Lady Eagles comprise the competitive cheer squad. Chesnee's Lilly Brown was named the All Upstate Competitive Cheerleader of the Year by the Greenville News, and teammate Miley Harris was named the AAA Cheerleader of the Year by the South Carolina High School League (SCHSL). Abigail Fogle has signed with the Limestone College dance team, and Rebecca Scruggs has signed with Presbyterian College competitive cheer team; and

 

Whereas, this year Chesnee's competitive cheer captured the program's fifth state championship, most recently claiming the title in 2019. The Lady Eagles were runners-up in 2022 and 2023; and

 

Whereas, in a sport that exacts precision, grace, and strength, Head Coach Allie Stubbs and her skilled coaching staff capitalized on their own athletic experience and training to forge a championship-caliber team and teach these athletes life principles that will endure beyond the mats; and

 

Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives values the pride and recognition that the Chesnee competitive cheerleaders have brought to their school and their community, and the members look forward to following their continued achievements in the days ahead. Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:

 

That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor the Chesnee High School competitive cheer squad, coaches, and school officials for an outstanding season and congratulate them for winning the 2024 South Carolina Class AAA State Championship title.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Principal Erik Gerstenacker and Coach Allie Stubbs.

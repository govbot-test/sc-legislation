A senate RESOLUTION

 

TO CONGRATULATE James Denny UPON THE OCCASION OF HIS RETIREMENT, TO COMMEND HIM FOR HIS forty YEARS OF DEDICATED SERVICE, AND TO WISH HIM MUCH HAPPINESS AND FULFILLMENT IN THE YEARS AHEAD.

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate are pleased to recognize James Denny upon the occasion of his retirement from the city of Cayce's Parks Department; and

 

Whereas, Mr. Denny has served as the Parks Director for the city of Cayce for forty years. He has served the Cayce community with distinction, playing a pivotal role in enhancing the city's parks system, expanding recreational opportunities, and creating welcoming spaces for residents of all ages. Under his leadership, Cayce has seen continued investment in its parks and facilities, strengthening quality of life and community pride; and

 

Whereas, during his tenure, Mr. Denny championed events that brought families together and highlighted the natural beauty of Cayce, such as Cayce Beautification Foundation projects, Christmas in Cayce, the Soiree on State, the Cayce Riverwalk, and numerous park enhancements. His commitment to maintaining high-quality recreational spaces has left a lasting impact on the community; and

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate appreciate the passion and dedication that James Denny has shown in serving the people and the State of South Carolina.  Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the Senate:

 

That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, congratulate James Denny upon the occasion of his retirement, commend him for his forty years of dedicated service, and wish him much happiness and fulfillment in the years ahead.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to James Denny.

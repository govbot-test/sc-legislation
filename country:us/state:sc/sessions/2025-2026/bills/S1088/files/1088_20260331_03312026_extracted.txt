A senate RESOLUTION TO SALUTE THE PINEVIEW 6U ROOKIE BALL ALL-STAR BASEBALL TEAM AND COACHES FOR AN OUTSTANDING SEASON AND TO CONGRATULATE THEM ON CAPTURING THE 2025 DIAMOND YOUTH WORLD SERIES CHAMPIONSHIP TITLE. Whereas, baseball is a game of speed, power, precision, and agility, but to excel in baseball, a team must combine these talents with teamwork and good sportsmanship; and Whereas, uniting all these attributes in one unstoppable team, the players of the Pineview 6U Rookie Ball All-Star baseball team claimed the crown at the 2025 Diamond Youth World Series Championship, held in Dunn, North Carolina, on July 28, 2025; and Whereas, during the season, the All-Stars were coached to championship caliber by Brandon Waring, Brandon Corley, David Stoquert, and David Yoder. Along the way, these dedicated coaches saw their players win the District 4 DII and state DII tournaments in rookie ball, after which they watched, with much satisfaction, as their players carried that momentum into the world series and continued to play with a high level of confidence and toughness all the way to the top of the mountain. There, the All-Stars picked up hardware that only champions get to take home; and Whereas, the Pineview 6U All-Stars went a perfect 7-0 in the world series, outscoring opponents 110-34 with wins over teams from Mississippi, Alabama, North Carolina, Georgia, Virginia (twice), and Texas. Defined by elite defense and a relentless hitting attack, the team posted a 17-0 record across all postseason play, scoring 303 runs while allowing just 89; and Whereas, every player contributed at a high level throughout the summer. Offensively, Pineview was led by standout hitters Sully Corley, Rylan Ailstock, Colton Owens, and Kash Davis. The infield anchored the defense with consistent play from second baseman Easton Stoquert, third baseman Finnley Overcash, and pitcher Brenn Waring. In the outfield, Micah Peters, Austin Mackles, Bryson Renwrick, Hudson Yoder, and Jackson Owens came through with big catches, heads-up plays, and aggressive base running; and Whereas, it is with great pleasure that the Senate pauses to recognize the accomplishments and commitment of these fine athletes and their coaches and to congratulate them on their success. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate: That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, salute the Pineview 6U Rookie Ball All-Star baseball team and coaches for an outstanding season and congratulate them on capturing the 2025 Diamond Youth World Series Championship title. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Coaches Brandon Waring, Brandon Corley, David Stoquert, and David Yoder for the Pineview 6U Rookie Ball All-Star baseball team. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 31, 2026 at 04:19 PM

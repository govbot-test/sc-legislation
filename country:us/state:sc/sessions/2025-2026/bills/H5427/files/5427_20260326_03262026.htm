A house RESOLUTION

 

TO RECOGNIZE AND CELEBRATE THE DARLINGTON COUNTRY CLUB ON THE OCCASION OF ITS CENTENNIAL CELEBRATIONS AND TO HONOR ITS SIGNIFICANT CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE DARLINGTON COUNTY COMMUNITY.

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives were pleased to hear about the 100th anniversary of the Darlington County Country Club; and

 

Whereas, Darlington Country Club was founded in 1926 under Club President D.T. McKeithan Jr., and has been the foundation of the Pee Dee region's social and recreational life for generations. Over the last 100 years, the club has grown from a modest nine-hole course into a vibrant club steeped in tradition. Today, they offer golf, tennis, pickleball, dining, events, and family-focused amenities that continue to evolve with the community it serves. The current owners and operators are Tim and Nancy Huntley and Mrs. Huntley is active in the club's day-to-day operations and serves in a leadership and management role at the club; and

 

Whereas, the 2026 Centennial Celebration features a calendar full of events, including a Centennial Gala for members and their guests, a member-member golf tournament, a weekend and Legacy Dinner, a community open house and family day, a Memorial Day Centennial Celebration, a July 4 Centennial Celebration, a Labor Day weekend centennial celebration, as well as various other family and youth events throughout the year; and

 

Whereas, Nucor Steel South Carolina-North America's largest steel manufacturer and recycler- will play a vital role in supporting the many activities and experiences throughout this year-long celebration, sharing Darlington Country Club's commitment to positively impacting the daily lives of those in their community. As the presenting sponsor of the club's centennial celebrations, Nucor will also support Friends of Southern 500 Junior Golf, an organization formed from the Southern 500 Golf Invitational, that is dedicated to developing and inspiring young golfers in the Darlington community and surrounding areas. Through access to educational programs, competitive opportunities, and valuable resources, the organization fosters personal growth, sportsmanship, and a lifelong love for the game; and

 

Whereas, Darlington Country Club has become a cornerstone of the community, offering a wealth of amenities. These amenities include a challenging 18-hole course along Black Creek, a spacious and recently renovated clubhouse, an Olympic-sized swimming pool, tennis and pickleball courts, versatile dining, and meeting facilities; and

 

Whereas, for a century the Darlington Country Club has been a space for community, not only as a gathering place for friends and family, but also a place that fosters new relationships. Throughout the years, the club has been a spot where people come together for recreational fun, to celebrate big life events, and for fellowship with others; and

 

Whereas, through its unwavering commitment to the community of Darlington County and the surrounding areas, the Darlington Country Club has created a lasting legacy that will continue to enhance the quality of life in the community that they serve for many years to come. Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:

 

That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and celebrate the Darlington Country Club on the occasion of its centennial celebrations and honor its significant contributions to the Darlington County community.

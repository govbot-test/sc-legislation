Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 56-5-1538(H) of the S.C. Code is amended to read: (H)(1) A person who violates the provisions of this section is guilty of the misdemeanor of endangering emergency services personnel or operators of authorized emergency vehicles and, upon conviction, must be fined not less than three hundred dollars nor more than five hundred dollars. (2) Notwithstanding another provision of law, a person charged with a violation of this section is eligible to enroll in a Traffic Education Program (TEP). SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 10, 2026 at 1:28 PM

A senate RESOLUTION TO RECOGNIZE AND HONOR Tim Todd for winning the South Carolina Parks, Recreation, and Tourism's Fred Brinkman Award And to celebrate his outstanding Contributions to tourism in the state. Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate are pleased to recognize Tim Todd for winning this award while attending his fortieth Governor's Conference; and Whereas, this award is named after Fred P. Brinkman, South Carolina Parks, Recreation, and Tourism's longest serving director, who spent more than forty years building and shaping tourism in South Carolina. Brinkman Award winners are celebrated for advancing South Carolina's tourism industry in ways that will endure for generations. Since it was created in 1991, only nine other individuals have received the Brinkman award; and Whereas, a native of Walhalla and an alumnus of Clemson University, Mr. Todd devoted his career to tourism marketing in the Upstate. He has served as the executive director of Discover Upcountry Carolina since 1989, shaping the State's tourism industry through his collaborative spirit, genuine love for his home, and an unwavering commitment to helping visitors create treasured memories; and Whereas, although Mr. Todd has played a crucial role in growing tourism in Greenville and the Upstate, he recognizes that tourism is a statewide industry and believes in supporting every region of South Carolina. Mr. Todd is an active member of the South Carolina Association of Tourism Regions, serving as past president, chairman of the legislative committee, and secretary; and Whereas, beyond the State, he is active in the Southeastern Tourism Society, serving in various leadership roles. His dedication was recognized when he received the Dorothy Hardman "Spirit of STS" Award; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate greatly appreciate the dedication and commitment that Tim Todd has shown in serving the people and the State of South Carolina.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate: That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, recognize and honor Tim Todd for winning the South Carolina Parks, Recreation, and Tourism's Fred Brinkman award and celebrate his outstanding contributions to tourism in the State. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Tim Todd. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on April 01, 2026 at 06:14 PM

Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) Indicates Matter Stricken Indicates New Matter As Adopted By The Senate May 7, 2026 H. 5537 Introduced by Rep. Hayes S. Printed 5/7/26--S. Read the first time April 28, 2026 ________ A joint Resolution To Direct the Department of Administration, the State Fiscal Accountability Authority, or the appropriate agency, to transfer the National Guard armory in Dillon County, South Carolina, to the City of Dillon. Amend Title To Conform Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Notwithstanding Sections 1-11-58 and 25-1-1660 of the S.C. Code, the Department of Administration, the State Fiscal Accountability Authority, or the appropriate agency, is directed to transfer ownership of the National Guard Armory located at 1119 SC-34 in Dillon County, South Carolina, to the City of Dillon. SECTION 2.  Notwithstanding Sections 1-11-58 and 25-1-1660 of the S.C. Code, the Department of Administration, the State Fiscal Accountability Authority, or the appropriate agency, is directed to transfer ownership of the National Guard Armory located at 620 John G. Rose Drive in Timmonsville, South Carolina, to the City of Timmonsville. SECTION 3.  Notwithstanding Sections 1-11-58 and 25-1-1660 of the S.C. Code, the Department of Administration, the State Fiscal Accountability Authority, or the appropriate agency, is directed to transfer ownership of the National Guard Armory located at 10233 Marlboro Avenue, Barnwell, South Carolina, to Barnwell County. SECTION 4.  This joint resolution takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 07, 2026 at 01:41 PM

2025-2026 Bill 5537 Text of Previous Version (May. 05, 2026) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Bill 5537Indicates Matter StrickenIndicates New Matter(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) Committee Report May 5, 2026 H. 5537 Introduced by Rep. Hayes S. Printed 5/5/26--S. Read the first time April 28, 2026 ________ The committee on Senate Finance To whom was referred a Joint Resolution (H. 5537) to direct the Department of Administration, the State Fiscal Accountability Authority, or the appropriate agency, to transfer the National Guard Armory in Dillon, etc., respectfully Report: That they have duly and carefully considered the same, and recommend that the same do pass: HARVEY PEELER for Committee. _______ A joint Resolution To Direct the Department of Administration, the State Fiscal Accountability Authority, or the appropriate agency, to transfer the National Guard armory in Dillon County, South Carolina, to the City of Dillon. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Notwithstanding Sections 1-11-58 and 25-1-1660 of the S.C. Code, the Department of Administration, the State Fiscal Accountability Authority, or the appropriate agency, is directed to transfer ownership of the National Guard Armory located at 1119 SC-34 in Dillon County, South Carolina, to the City of Dillon. SECTION 2.  This joint resolution takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 05, 2026 at 09:58 PM

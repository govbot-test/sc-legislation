A house RESOLUTION TO RECOGNIZE AND HONOR THE RIDGE VIEW HIGH SCHOOL BOYS TRACK AND FIELD TEAM upon WINNING THE 2025 AAAAA DIVISION ONE STATE CHAMPIONSHIP TITLE AND TO CONGRATULATE THE TEAM'S OUTSTANDING PLAYERS, COACHES, AND STAFF FOR AN EXCEPTIONAL SEASON. Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is pleased to learn that the members of the Ridge View High School boys track and field team of Richland County captured the 2025 AAAAA Division One State Championship title at the South Carolina High School League track and field championships held in Harry Parone Stadium at Spring Valley High School on May 17, 2025; and Whereas, to the delight of their exhilarated fans, the Ridge View Blazers defeated an impressive, competitive field of teams. While in close competition with Spartanburg High School throughout the meet, the Blazers were able to overcome the Spartanburg Vikings score of 87.5 points with a total score of 91 points, winning by 3.5 points; and Whereas, a year ago the Blazers won the AAAA State Championship. This year the Ridge View boys team moved up to the AAAAA Division One. The step up in competition did not prevent the team and Head Coach Charles Proctor from winning their second consecutive championship; and Whereas, the Ridge View Blazers competed in almost every event, even doubling up in several events giving them the depth and opportunity to prevail over their competitors. The Blazers had some outstanding performances, placing in most events and finished with two gold medals. Jordan Best won state in the 100-meter dash, finishing with a time of 10.52 seconds. Quency Grant, Khristian Jackson, Mounir Spann, and Jordan Best won the 4x100 relay with a time of 40.88 seconds; and Whereas, success at this level is the result of relentless work, demanding practices, team effort, and sportsmanship. A testament to the teamwork and determined efforts put forth by the staff and runners alike to continue to grow and improve their program. In the past four years, the Ridge View boys track and field team has won three regional championships and two state titles; and Whereas, in a sport that demands strength, speed, and tireless effort, Head Coach Charles Proctor and his skilled coaching staff utilized their own experience, passion, and training to forge a championship-caliber team and teach these athletes life principles that will endure beyond the track and field; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives appreciate the pride and recognition that the Ridge View boys track and field team have brought to their school and community. The House is honored to recognize these outstanding student athletes for their accomplishments both on and off the track and field. The members look forward to following their future achievements in the days to come. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor the Ridge View High School boys track and field team upon winning the 2025 AAAAA Division One State Championship title and to congratulate the team's outstanding players, coaches, and staff for an exceptional season. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Principal and Head Coach Charles Proctor of Ridge View High School. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 14, 2026 at 04:02 PM

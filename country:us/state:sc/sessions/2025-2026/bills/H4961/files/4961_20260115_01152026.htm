A concurrent RESOLUTION

 

To congratulate the t.l. hanna boys golf team and coaches on their impressive win of the 2024-2025 south carolina high school league aaaaa division II state championship title and applaud them on a fantastic season.

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina General Assembly were pleased to hear that on May 20, 2025, at the Cliffs Mountain Course in Greenville, the T.L. Hanna boys golf team won their third consecutive state championship title. The Yellow Jackets were favored going into the match, but they were up against stiff competition. The T.L. Hanna golfers knew that they had to work hard and concentrate to defend their state title; and

 

Whereas, the Yellow Jackets had an impressive performance at the two-day state match on May 19th and 20th, leading throughout the match. They were closely trailed by second place finisher Greenville High School up until the end. T.L. Hanna bested Greenville by one shot on the final day, finishing with a score of 570. Greenville came up short and tied for second place with Riverside; and

 

Whereas, T.L. Hanna's Eric Erlenkeuser also took home the individual state title. After an impressive performance throughout both rounds, at the end of regulation he was tied for the first-place spot with three other golfers. After competing in a fierce playoff to determine the winner, Erlenkeuser was able to capture the individual medal for the 2024-2025 AAAAA Division II State Championship; and

 

Whereas, in a sport that requires precision, concentration, and mental strength, Head Coach Eric Bona and his dedicated staff utilized their own experiences and passion for the sport to craft a championship-caliber team. The discipline and life lessons that he and his staff have taught their team will continue to shape them into well-rounded student athletes on and off the course; and

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina General Assembly value the pride and recognition that the T.L. Hanna boys golf team has brought to their school and community. The members look forward to hearing about the future accomplishments of these young athletes. Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives, the Senate concurring:

 

That the members of the South Carolina General Assembly, by this resolution, congratulate the T. L. Hanna boys golf team and coaches on their impressive win of the 2024-2025 South Carolina High School League AAAAA Division II State Championship title and applaud them on a fantastic season.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to T.L. Hanna Principal Walter Mayfield and Head Coach Eric Bona.

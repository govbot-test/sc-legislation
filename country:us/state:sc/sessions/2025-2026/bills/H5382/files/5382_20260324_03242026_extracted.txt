2025-2026 Bill 5382 Text of Previous Version (Mar. 24, 2026) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Bill 5382Indicates Matter StrickenIndicates New Matter(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) A house RESOLUTION TO RECOGNIZE AND HONOR S.L. BARNO FUNERAL DIRECTORS FOR THE SERVICES THEY PROVIDE FAMILIES IN DARLINGTON AND SURROUNDING COMMUNITIES. Whereas, founded by Sherman L. Barno, S.L. Barno Funeral Directors is a family-owned funeral service provider that serves families in Darlington and surrounding communities with dignity, compassion, and professionalism, and represents the continuation of a long tradition of service to families during their most difficult moments; and Whereas, Sherman Barno built his reputation in the funeral service profession through years of experience working within the Darlington area funeral industry. His dedication to caring for families and honoring the lives of the deceased eventually led him to establish his own funeral service organization; and Whereas, in 2018, he formally opened S.L. Barno Funeral Directors in Darlington, creating a locally-owned business committed to personalized funeral services and community support; and Whereas, located in the heart of Darlington, the funeral home was established with the goal of providing respectful, individualized services that reflect the traditions, faith, and cultural values of the families it serves; and Whereas, S.L. Barno Funeral Directors has become known for compassionate care and attention to detail, including the way it assists with traditional funeral services, memorial celebrations, burial arrangements, and grief support; and Whereas, now under the leadership of Sherman L. Barno Jr. who serves as owner and manager, the funeral home continues to build upon the vision of its founder. The organization strives to uphold a spirit of excellence in funeral service while remaining deeply connected to the Darlington community; and Whereas, since its founding, S.L. Barno Funeral Directors has assisted numerous families throughout Darlington County, helping them celebrate the lives and legacies of loved ones while providing comfort and guidance during times of loss; and Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives values the important role of S.L. Barno Funeral Directors in the community's tradition of honoring life and preserving memory, through a commitment to service, professionalism, and respect. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor S.L. Barno Funeral Directors for the services they provide families in Darlington and surrounding communities. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to S.L. Barno Funeral Directors. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 24, 2026 at 01:14 PM

A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY AMENDING SECTION 12-36-2120, RELATING TO SALES TAX EXEMPTIONS SO AS TO PROVIDE A SALES TAX EXEMPTION FOR DIAPERS AND TOILET PAPER. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 12-36-2120 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: (85) diapers and toilet paper. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor and applies to tax years beginning after 2024. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on December 06, 2024 at 11:24 AM

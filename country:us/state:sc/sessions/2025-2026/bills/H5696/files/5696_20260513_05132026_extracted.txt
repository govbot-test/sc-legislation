A house RESOLUTION TO RECOGNIZE AND HONOR KATHY HILL FOR MORE THAN THIRTY-FIVE YEARS OF DEDICATED PUBLIC SERVICE TO THE STATE OF SOUTH CAROLINA AND THE CITY AND COUNTY OF SPARTANBURG, AND TO COMMEND HER FOR HER EXEMPLARY CIVIC LEADERSHIP AND ENDURING CONTRIBUTIONS TO HER COMMUNITY. Whereas, Kathy Hill, a native of Lyman, has devoted more than thirty-five years of distinguished service to the State of South Carolina and the citizens of the City and County of Spartanburg, demonstrating unwavering dedication, professionalism, and commitment to public service; and Whereas, a graduate of James F. Byrnes High School in Duncan, Mrs. Hill earned an associate degree from Spartanburg Community College and later obtained a Bachelor of Arts degree in Interdisciplinary Studies with a concentration in nonprofit administration from the University of South Carolina Upstate, exemplifying her lifelong pursuit of educational and professional excellence; and Whereas, Mrs. Hill began her career with the City of Spartanburg in housing services and subsequently advanced through positions in community development and executive assistant to the city manager before assuming her current role as community relations manager; and Whereas, even after retirement, Mrs. Hill returned to continue serving the residents of Spartanburg, reflecting her deep commitment to the people and communities she has faithfully supported throughout her career; and Whereas, during her tenure as community relations manager, Mrs. Hill provided leadership and oversight for numerous initiatives designed to strengthen neighborhoods and foster community engagement, including the city's annual Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Unity Week Celebration, National Night Out, Citizen Academy programs, neighborhood development initiatives, traffic-calming efforts, accommodations tax support, antilitter campaigns, and the Neighborhood Tool Shed program; and Whereas, through these initiatives, Mrs. Hill served as a vital bridge between local government and the citizens of Spartanburg, earning admiration, trust, and respect of residents throughout the community; and Whereas, Mrs. Hill's service extends beyond municipal government through her mentorship with Meeting Street Academy, service as a board member for Business and Professional Women and Keep Spartanburg Beautiful, participation as a graduate of Leadership Spartanburg, and her recent induction into the Top Ladies of Distinction, Greater Spartanburg Upstate GEMS Chapter. Her civic footprint reflects a woman who has invested herself fully in the betterment of her community; and Whereas, in her personal life, Kathy and her husband, Calvin Hill, are faithful members of Bethesda Baptist Church in Union, where she serves as a youth ministry advisor, deaconess, member of the mass choir, and a singer with the praise and worship ministry, Serenity.  She is a devoted sibling, proud aunt, and cherished godmother; and Whereas, guided by the scripture found in Colossians 3:23, "Work willingly at whatever you do, as though you were working for the Lord rather than the people," Mrs. Hill has exemplified selfless service, integrity, and compassion throughout her life and career; and Whereas, it is altogether fitting and proper for the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives to pause in their deliberations to commend Kathy Hill for her outstanding achievements, exemplary leadership, and unwavering commitment to service and excellence. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor Kathy Hill for more than thirty-five years of dedicated public service to the State of South Carolina and the City and County of Spartanburg, and commend her for her exemplary civic leadership and enduring contributions to her community. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Kathy Hill. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 13, 2026 at 11:01 AM

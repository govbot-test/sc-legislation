Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 12-36-2120(75) of the S.C. Code is amended to read: (75) unprepared food that lawfully may be purchased with United States Department of Agriculture food coupons, without regard to any demonstration project pursuant to United States Department of Agriculture regulations that would limit the use of food coupons on otherwise eligible items for a limited period of time. However, the exemption allowed by this item applies only to the state sales and use tax imposed pursuant to this chapter; SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 5, 2026 at 11:44 AM

John FultonHISTORY OF LEGISLATIVE ACTIONS Date Body Action Description with journal page number 5/13/2026 House Introduced and adopted (House Journal-page 5) View the latest legislative information at the websiteVERSIONS OF THIS BILL05/13/2026 A house RESOLUTION to recognize and honor Sergeant Major John Wesley Fulton, Retired, of Clarendon County and to express gratitude for his dedication to the Manning community. Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives are deeply appreciative of the dedication that John Fulton has provided to Manning and the surrounding area, serving as a pillar of strength, a mentor, and a guide; and Whereas, with demonstrable compassion, integrity, and commitment, he has served as a coach and assistant coach for the Manning Junior High School basketball team, helping to lead young athletes to several championships while instilling discipline, teamwork, and character into the lives of countless students; and Whereas, Sergeant Major Fulton has positively impacted generations of young people, encouraging them to pursue productive and purposeful lives. He and his family initiated the John Wesley Fulton Scholarship Fund offered every year to students who show academic promise and are willing to write an essay describing why they feel that they deserve the scholarship; and Whereas, beyond athletics, Sergeant Major Fulton has remained deeply involved in community service, devoting his personal time to assisting elderly residents, disabled individuals, and citizens living on fixed incomes by cutting lawns and providing support wherever needed; and Whereas, a faithful member of The Masters Place Ministries, he serves the congregation as a deacon and provides spiritual guidance, encouragement, and support to individuals and families throughout the community during difficult times; and Whereas, Sergeant Major Fulton serves as the president of the Tri-County Corvette Club. Under his leadership, the organization actively donates both time and financial support to critically ill individuals and families in need. The club regularly organizes Corvette drive-through events at the homes of those facing serious illnesses, bringing encouragement, hope, and joy during challenging times. The club provides donations to assist with medical expenses and other miscellaneous needs for struggling families within the community; and Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is deeply grateful for the impact of John Fulton's service, uplifting lives in the community, mentoring youth, and supporting countless individuals and families through the years. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor Sergeant Major John Wesley Fulton, Retired, of Clarendon County and express gratitude for his dedication to the Manning community. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Sergeant Major John Wesley Fulton. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 13, 2026 at 10:55 AM

"Chuck" Dawley, PLS, Owner of Dawley Surveying Company, LLC, and to congratulate him for being named the 2023-2024 surveyor of the year. Whereas, the South Carolina Senate is pleased to learn that Chuck Dawley, PLS was honored as the 2023-2024 Surveyor of the Year by the South Carolina Society of Professional Land Surveyors (SCSPLS); and Whereas, Mr. Dawley is the owner of Dawley Land Surveying, LLC in Wando. He became a licensed professional land surveyor in the State in 1982; and Whereas, Mr. Dawley has served in many capacities in the SCSPLS Coastal Chapter. He is currently serving on the SCSPLS Research Task Force and is an active member of the society; and Whereas, Mr. Dawley is committed to the growth and profession of land surveying. He is knowledgeable, dedicated to the profession, and a friend to all. His exemplary commitment to land surveying is ingrained in his interactions with clients and in his service to the society; and Whereas, the Senate is grateful for Mr. Dawley's dedicated service to the people and the State of South Carolina and takes great pleasure in saluting him for receiving the prestigious 2023-2024 Surveyor of the Year award.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate: That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, congratulate Charles F. "Chuck" Dawley, PLS, owner of Dawley Surveying Company, LLC of Wando, and congratulate him for being named the 2023-2024 Surveyor of the Year. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Chuck Dawley. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 26, 2024 at 12:12 PM

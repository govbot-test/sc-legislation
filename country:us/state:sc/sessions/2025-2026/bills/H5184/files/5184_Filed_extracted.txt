Smith, Spann-Wilder, Stavrinakis, Taylor, Teeple, Terribile, Vaughan, Waters, Weeks, Wetmore, White, Whitmire, Wickensimer, Williams, Willis, Wooten and YowDocument Path: LC-0527SA-JAH26.docxIntroduced in the House on February 17, 2026Adopted by the House on February 17, 2026Summary: Catawba Ridge Softball TeamHISTORY OF LEGISLATIVE ACTIONS Date Body Action Description with journal page number 2/17/2026 House Introduced and adopted (House Journal-page 4) View the latest legislative information at the websiteVERSIONS OF THIS BILL02/17/2026 A house RESOLUTION to congratulate the Catawba Ridge High School softball team, coaches, and school officials for an outstanding season and to honor them for winning the south carolina class AAAAA Division II Championship title. Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is pleased to recognize the Catawba Ridge High School softball team for capturing the South Carolina Class AAAAA Division II State Championship on May 29, 2025; and Whereas, under the outstanding leadership of Head Coach Michael Mayak and his dedicated coaching staff, the Copperheads compiled an exceptional 31-2 overall record, consistently demonstrating discipline, determination, and a steadfast commitment to excellence throughout the season; and Whereas, to the excitement and pride of their loyal supporters, the Catawba Ridge Copperheads defeated a highly talented St. James Sharks team by a score of 9-1 in the championship game, a decisive victory that reflected the team's perseverance, teamwork, and exemplary sportsmanship; and Whereas, the Copperheads earned a ranking of number one in the State of South Carolina and number twenty nationally by Sports Illustrated while securing their third consecutive state championship, and over the past three seasons, have amassed an extraordinary 76-7 record, produced eighteen All-Region selections, eight All-State honorees, and two South Carolina Players of the Year; and Whereas, during that same period, seven student athletes have advanced to play Division I or Division II collegiate softball, with two additional players set to compete at the Division I level following graduation this year; and Whereas, the championship roster includes players Peyton Batot, Camryn Bird, Charli Burney, Gabbey Cook, Jaidyn Harris, Aloni Hogans, Mia Kovacs, Kendra Murray, Keira O'Brien, Via Petri, Kyrsten Reynolds, Sarah Rocheleau, Taylan Sistare, Hannah Smith, and Brynlee Grace Yates, whose accomplishments were achieved under the leadership of Head Coach Michael Mayak, along with coaches Kyle Harris, and Josh Yates; and Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives takes great pride in honoring these exceptional student athletes, who have brought distinction to their school, community, and state through their remarkable achievements, and the members look forward with confidence to the team's continued success in the years to come.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, congratulate the Catawba Ridge High School Softball team, coaches, and school officials for an outstanding season and honor them for winning the South Carolina Class AAAAA Division II championship title. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Principal Barry Ledford and Head Coach Michael Mayak. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 17, 2026 at 12:54 PM

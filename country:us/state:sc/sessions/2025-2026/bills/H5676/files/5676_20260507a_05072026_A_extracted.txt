A house RESOLUTION to congratulate Kamille Whaley upon her graduation from Claflin University and TO WISH HER MUCH SUCCESS AND HAPPINESS IN ALL HER FUTURE ENDEAVORS. Whereas, Kamille Whaley, a native of Columbia, is a graduating senior at Claflin University, majoring in marketing with a minor in business management; and Whereas, Kamille has demonstrated outstanding academic achievement, maintaining a 3.8 GPA and earning membership in the Alice Carson Tisdale Honors College, where she has distinguished herself as both a scholar and campus leader; and Whereas, she currently serves as the 67th Student Government Association Executive President for the 2025-2026 Academic Year, following her impactful service as the 2024-2025 Student Government Association Chief of Staff; and Whereas, her leadership extends beyond student government through her role as an AmeriCorps VISTA Student Success Support Coordinator, where she actively advances initiatives that promote student achievement and retention; and Whereas, during the summer of 2024, Kamille interned with TD SYNNEX, where her excellence and innovation were recognized with the prestigious Future Innovators Award; and Whereas, Kamille exemplifies a strong commitment to service, scholarship, and sisterhood as a spring 2025 initiate of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority Incorporated, Gamma Chi Chapter; and Whereas, her dedication to combining academic excellence with meaningful service reflects her preparation to excel in both corporate and community environments; and Whereas, guided by resilience, purpose, and faith, Kamille is committed to creating opportunities that inspire others to lead with impact and strives to leave every space better than she found it; and Whereas, she embodies the principals reflected in Matthew 5:16, "In the same way, let your good deeds shine out for all to see, so that everyone will praise your heavenly Father"; and Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives take great pleasure in recognizing Kamille Whaley for her outstanding achievements, exemplary leadership, and unwavering commitment to service and excellence, and the members look forward to following her continued success with much interest and anticipation. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, congratulate Kamille Whaley upon her graduation from Claflin University and wish her much success and happiness in all her future endeavors. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Kamille Whaley. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 07, 2026 at 02:16 PM

A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY AMENDING SECTION 7-13-25, RELATING TO EARLY VOTING, SO AS TO DELETE PROVISIONS EXCLUDING PRESIDENTIAL PREFERENCE PRIMARIES FROM APPLICATIONS OF THIS SECTION. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 7-13-25(L) of the S.C. Code is amended to read: (L) The provisions of this section do not apply to presidential preference primaries held pursuant to Section 7-11-20. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on April 09, 2025 at 03:29 PM

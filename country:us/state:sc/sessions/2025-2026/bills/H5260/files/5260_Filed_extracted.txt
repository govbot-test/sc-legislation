Whereas, the largest student academic organization in the nation, the National Beta Club will observe March 9-13, 2026, as National Beta Club Week. In keeping with the national observance, it is entirely appropriate that this week should also be observed in the State of South Carolina; and Whereas, the members of the Muller Road Middle School Beta Club in Richland County School District Two are part of a national organization of more than five hundred thousand students from fourth- through twelfth-grade; and Whereas, these students are dedicated to serving in their schools and communities as they live out their organization's motto, "Let us lead by serving others"; and Whereas, the National Beta Club promotes the development of student leaders by striving for excellence in academics, service, character, and leadership; and Whereas, in striving for such excellence, the members of the Muller Road Middle School Beta Club provide service that has a positive impact on their community and its citizens. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, declare March 9-13, 2026, as Beta Club Week in South Carolina and encourage all citizens of the Palmetto State to learn more about, and support, the national Beta Club, whose members are preparing themselves to be better, more responsible citizens as they provide meaningful service to their respective schools and communities. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the Muller Road Middle School Beta Club. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 25, 2026 at 11:32 AM

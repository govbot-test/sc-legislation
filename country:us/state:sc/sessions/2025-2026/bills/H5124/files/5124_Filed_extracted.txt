Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives were pleased to learn that Kelli Overcash, principal of Kershaw Elementary School in Lancaster County, has been named the 2026 South Carolina Elementary School Principal of the Year by the South Carolina Association of School Administrators; and Whereas, every year, three remarkable South Carolina principals are selected for South Carolina Principal of the Year awards, one each at the elementary, middle, and high school levels. Mrs. Overcash was chosen for this honor from an outstanding field of accomplished administrative candidates by a panel of expert judges; and Whereas, Mrs. Overcash, an inspiring leader, has served Kershaw Elementary School for nearly a decade, leading with a people-first approach in her role as principal by championing teachers and staff, empowering every child, and ensuring that all students are seen, heard, and challenged to reach their full potential regardless of circumstance; and Whereas, during her tenure, Mrs. Overcash has achieved significant accomplishments for Kershaw Elementary School, including improved staff retention and the school earning three consecutive years of "Excellent" ratings from the South Carolina State Department of Education, reflecting her steadfast commitment to student academic growth and meaningful community engagement; and Whereas, this well-deserved honor marks a first for Lancaster County Schools, reflecting the district's continued commitment to excellence in education and school leadership, and its dedication to the overall growth and success of all students; and Whereas, Mrs. Overcash, a steadfast advocate for equity and opportunity, is not only an exceptional educator but also a respected leader within the community, having been named the inaugural recipient of the 2025 Lancaster County School District Principal of the Year award for her outstanding leadership, vision, and compassion, with academic achievement improving significantly under her guidance; and Whereas, for her devoted service to the students at Kershaw Elementary School, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives are pleased to applaud Mrs. Overcash on her selection as the 2026 South Carolina Elementary School Principal of the Year, and congratulate her on the pride and recognition that she has brought to her school and community. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor educator Kelli Overcash of Kershaw Elementary School in Lancaster County, congratulate her upon being named the 2026 South Carolina Elementary School Principal of the Year, and commend her for her dedicated service to education in this great State. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Kelli Overcash. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 10, 2026 at 1:05 PM

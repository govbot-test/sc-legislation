A house RESOLUTION To AUTHORIZE THE SOUTH CAROLINA INDEPENDENT SCHOOL ASSOCIATION (SCISA) TO USE THE CHAMBER OF THE SOUTH CAROLINA HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES FOR ITS STUDENT GOVERNMENT FALL CONFERENCE ON MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 14, 2026, AND TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 15, 2026, PROVIDED THE HOUSE IS NOT IN SESSION, AND THE CHAMBER MAY NOT BE USED IF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES IS IN SESSION OR IF THE CHAMBER IS OTHERWISE UNAVAILABLE. Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, authorize the South Carolina Independent School Association (SCISA) to use the chamber of the South Carolina House of Representatives for its Student Government Fall Conference on Monday, September 14, 2026, and Tuesday, September 15, 2026, provided the House is not in session, and the chamber may not be used if the House of Representatives is in session or if the chamber is otherwise unavailable. Be it further resolved that the State House security forces shall provide assistance and access as necessary for this meeting in accordance with previous procedures. Be it further resolved that no charges may be made for the use of the House chamber by SCISA on these dates. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 14, 2026 at 03:58 PM

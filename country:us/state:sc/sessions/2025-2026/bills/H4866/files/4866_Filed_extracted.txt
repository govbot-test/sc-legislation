Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives are pleased to learn that the members of the Ninety Six High School marching band of Greenwood County took top honors at the state competition held at North Central High School on Saturday, November 1, 2025; and Whereas, the Ninety Six High School marching band secured its thirteenth state title, their fourth consecutive win, with a spectacular show entitled, "The World's Our Stage" receiving an impressive score of 84.95; and Whereas, Band Director David Vickery and his staff skillfully utilized their own experience and passion to build a championship-caliber band and impart life principles that will prove invaluable throughout these young musicians' lives on and off the parade ground; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives value the pride and recognition that the Ninety Six High School band members have brought to their school and community and look forward to following their continued accomplishments in the days ahead. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor the Ninety Six High School Marching Band, band director, and school officials for another remarkable season and congratulate them for winning the 2025 South Carolina Band Directors Association Class A State Championship Title. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Ninety Six High School Principal Fitz Sprowl and Band Director David Vickery. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 14, 2026 at 3:34 PM

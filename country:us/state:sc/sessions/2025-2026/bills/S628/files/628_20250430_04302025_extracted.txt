Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) A concurrent RESOLUTION to authorize palmetto boys state to use the senate and house chambers on friday, june 13, 2025. Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring: That the members of the South Carolina General Assembly, by this resolution, hereby authorize Palmetto Boys State to use the Chambers of the House of Representatives and the Senate on Friday, June 13, 2025, for its annual State House meeting. If either House is in statewide session, then the Chamber of that House may not be used.

Davis OF Fairfield COUNTY AND TO EXTEND THEIR DEEPEST SYMPATHY TO HIS LOVING FAMILY AND HIS MANY FRIENDS. Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives were saddened to learn of the death of Robert W. Davis at the age of seventy-seven on July 17, 2025, surrounded by his devoted family; and Whereas, born in Fairfield County on February 6, 1948, Mr. Davis was deeply committed to his family, his community, and his faith; and Whereas, a graduate of Fairfield High School, he held the position of vice president of the alumni association and remained engaged in and committed to preserving the history and progress of the school he loved; and Whereas, Mr. Davis served honorably in the United States Army and continued his service to others through a life dedicated to leadership and community improvement; and Whereas, a faithful member of his church, Greater St. John African Methodist Episcopal Zion Church, he previously served the congregation as a steward, offering both spiritual and practical guidance; and Whereas, Mr. Davis served as a Fairfield County Council member for eighteen years, leaving a legacy of advocacy, growth, and integrity; and Whereas, professionally, he served as a telecommunications expert until his retirement, and he also found success in the world of real estate with a passion for helping others find their place in the world; and Whereas, Mr. Davis was a devoted husband, a loving father, a proud grandfather, and a cherished father-in-law, affectionately referring to his daughters-in-law as "his girls"; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives are grateful for the life and legacy of Robert Davis and for the example of love, service, and strength he set for all who knew him. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, express their profound sorrow upon the passing of Robert W. Davis of Fairfield County and extend their deepest sympathy to his loving family and his many friends. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the family of Robert W. Davis. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 14, 2026 at 4:12 PM

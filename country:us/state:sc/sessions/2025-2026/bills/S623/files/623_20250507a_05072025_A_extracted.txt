2025-2026 Bill 623 Text of Previous Version (May. 07, 2025) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Bill 623Indicates Matter StrickenIndicates New Matter(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) AMENDED May 7, 2025 S. 623 Introduced by Senator Goldfinch L. Printed 5/7/25--H. Read the first time May 1, 2025 ________ A bill TO EXEMPT GEORGETOWN COUNTY FROM CERTAIN BUILDING REQUIREMENTS AND TO ALLOW THE COUNTY TO INSTEAD ENFORCE AE standards in GEORGETOWN COUNTY'S FLOOD DAMAGE PREVENTION ORDINANCE. Amend Title To Conform Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1. Georgetown County is exempt from building requirements for properties within, or affected by, the limit of moderate wave action line as shown on the May 9, 2023, flood insurance rate map and may enforce AE standards as set forth in Georgetown County's Flood Damage Prevention ordinance and the current state building code. SECTION 2. The provisions of this act are repealed upon the adoption of the next limit of moderate wave action line. SECTION 3.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 07, 2025 at 02:37 PM

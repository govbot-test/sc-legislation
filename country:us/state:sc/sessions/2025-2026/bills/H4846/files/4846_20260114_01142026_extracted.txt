A house RESOLUTION TO CELEBRATE THE LEXINGTON HIGH SCHOOL GIRLS GOLF TEAM ON WINNING THE 2025 SOUTH CAROLINA HIGH SCHOOL LEAGUE CLASS AAAAA DIVISION I STATE CHAMPIONSHIP TITLE. Whereas, on October 28, 2025, the members of the Lexington High School girls golf team packed up an impressive first-place trophy, the coveted proof of their victory at the 2025 South Carolina High School League Class AAAAA Division I State Championship tournament, held at Carolina Country Club in Spartanburg; and Whereas, the victory put Lexington back on top of the girls golf podium in South Carolina and gave the team its fifteenth state title; and Whereas, the Wildcats finished with a score of 336 through 18 holes of match play. Rainy conditions shortened the competition to a single round, giving Lexington a one-stroke win over runner-up Dorman; and Whereas, with that fifteenth title in hand, Lexington continues to represent the gold standard for girls golf teams in South Carolina. The Wildcats have ten more titles than the next-highest team and won seven in a row from 2004 to 2010. This year, the team fed off its motivation from last season's third-place finish and snapped a four-year championship drought; and Whereas, to put the icing on the cake, three Wildcats received All-State honors for their performance at the championship. While Lexington didn't have the top finishers, their depth helped deliver them a title. Lyles Herritage finished with a score of 82, Giavanna Martusciello shot an 84, and Claire Kim also had 84; and Whereas, in a sport that demands the patient exercise of polished skill, Head Coach Brandon Smith, strongly supported by Assistant Coach Greg Alrasian, used their own athletic abilities and training to build a championship team and teach these athletes lessons that will prove invaluable throughout life, both on and off the fairways and greens; and Whereas, the House of Representatives takes great pleasure in saluting the Wildcats on a fine season, and the members look forward to hearing of their further accomplishments in the days ahead. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, celebrate the Lexington High School girls golf team on winning the 2025 South Carolina High School League Class AAAAA Division I State Championship title. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Principal Jennifer McNair and Head Coach Brandon Smith of Lexington High School. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 14, 2026 at 03:02 PM

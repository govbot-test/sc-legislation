2025-2026 Bill 762 Text of Previous Version (Jan. 13, 2026) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Bill 762Indicates Matter StrickenIndicates New Matter(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) A Senate RESOLUTION TO CONGRATULATE Reverend Kenneth C. Taylor UPON THE OCCASION OF HIS eighteenth ANNIVERSARY AS PASTOR OF Turner Memorial AME Church AND TO COMMEND HIM FOR HIS MANY YEARS OF SERVICE TO HIS CONGREGATION AND THE West Columbia COMMUNITY. Whereas, the South Carolina Senate is proud to recognize Reverend Kenneth C. Taylor upon the occasion of his eighteenth pastoral anniversary; and Whereas, Reverend Taylor serves as the pastor of Turner Memorial AME Church in West Columbia. His church recognizes him as a phenomenal pastor who is dedicated to his calling and the members of his church community. He leads with empathy, understanding, and compassion; and Whereas, Reverend Taylor has established several programs that continue to flourish, including the Food Ministry, Clothes Ministry, Youth Program, nightly Prayer Ministry, and a free medical clinic that provides free medical services from a licensed physician, among other ministries and services; and Whereas, a beloved leader in his community, Reverend Taylor is highly regarded as a builder of bridges between people, drawing members of the community to the church and its ministry work; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate greatly appreciate the ministry of Reverend Taylor and his service to Turner Memorial AME Church..  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate: That the members of the Senate, by this resolution, congratulate Reverend Kenneth C. Taylor upon the occasion of his eighteenth anniversary as pastor of Turner Memorial AME Church and commend him for his many years of service to his congregation and the West Columbia community. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Reverend Kenneth C. Taylor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 13, 2026 at 12:01 PM

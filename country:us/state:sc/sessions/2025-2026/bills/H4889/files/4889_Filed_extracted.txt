Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives are pleased to learn that Ashley Burnett has been named to the South Carolina Baseball Coaches Hall of Fame; and Whereas, Mr. Burnett graduated from Lexington High School and played baseball for Anderson University and Winthrop University; and Whereas, he coached baseball at Aynor High School and at Marlboro County High School in Bennettsville for one year each and three years at Emerald High School in Greenwood before coming to Gilbert High School in 2006; and Whereas, Coach Burnett is the head coach for Gilbert High School baseball and has been coaching with the school's baseball program for the past twenty-five years, and he has led the baseball team to state championships in 2006, 2008, 2012, and 2021; and Whereas, in April 2019, he reached a milestone, recording his five hundredth career victory when Gilbert defeated Aiken High at the Sandlapper Classic. In honor of that extraordinary event, he was presented with a baseball jersey reading "Burnett 500" and was dubbed Mister 500; and Whereas, together with his beloved wife, Shannon, Coach Burnett has reared two fine children, Alexandria and Abigail; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives join with family and friends to honor Ashley Burnett upon the occasion of his prestigious induction into the South Carolina Baseball Coaches Hall of Fame. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor Ashley Burnett, Athletic Director at Gilbert High School, for his outstanding career as a coach and congratulate him for his induction into the South Carolina Baseball Coaches Hall of Fame. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Athletic Director Ashley Burnett. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 14, 2026 at 4:06 PM

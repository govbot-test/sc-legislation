2025-2026 Bill 1124 Text of Previous Version (Apr. 15, 2026) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Bill 1124Indicates Matter StrickenIndicates New Matter(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) A senate RESOLUTION TO RECOGNIZE APRIL 2026 AS "Foot health Awareness MONTH" IN SOUTH CAROLINA. Whereas, the human foot is a complex structure containing twenty-six bones and serves as the foundation for the rest of the body's mobility and overall health; and Whereas, approximately twenty percent of Americans experience at least one foot problem annually, and many systemic diseases, such as diabetes and peripheral arterial disease, often manifest first in the feet; and Whereas, untreated foot conditions can lead to further complications in the ankles, knees, hips, and lower back, affecting quality of life for many South Carolinians; and Whereas, National Foot Health Awareness Month, established by the American Podiatric Medical Association, seeks to educate the public on simple, preventative steps such as daily inspections, proper footwear, and regular check-ups to avoid serious injury and illness; and Whereas, it is the desire of the South Carolina General Assembly to encourage its citizens to prioritize their health and well-being from the ground up.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate: That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, recognize April 2026 as "Foot Health Awareness Month" in South Carolina. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the South Carolina Podiatric Medical Association. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on April 15, 2026 at 03:17 PM

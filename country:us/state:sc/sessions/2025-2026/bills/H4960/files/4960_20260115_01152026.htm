A concurrent RESOLUTION

 

TO CONGRATULATE THE T.L. HANNA GIRLS GOLF TEAM AND COACHES ON THEIR IMPRESSIVE WIN OF THE 2025-2026 SOUTH CAROLINA HIGH SCHOOL LEAGUE AAAAA DIVISION II STATE CHAMPIONSHIP TITLE AND APPLAUD THEM ON A FANTASTIC SEASON.

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina General Assembly are pleased to learn that the T.L. Hanna girls golf team won their second consecutive Class AAAAA Division II girls golf State Championship on October 28, 2025, on the Hackler Course at Coastal Carolina University; and

 

Whereas, despite wind and rain, the Yellow Jackets' composure allowed them to prevail, defending their title and finishing with an overall score of 642, besting both Conway, who finished second with a score of 654, and West Florence, who finished third with a score of 660; and

 

Whereas, the Lady Yellow Jackets showed grit and determination in the face of an uncertain tournament. Between worsening weather conditions and stiff competition, the team had to give it their all to defend their championship title. After finding themselves behind Chapin in the first round, the weather stilled, giving them the chance they needed in the second round to turn their game around. Some outstanding performances early in the second round from the leading golfers on the team helped everyone to play like champions. Coach Gabby Kitts knew that as long as they played a smart, safe game it would lead to their eventual win; and

 

Whereas, the T.L. Hanna Lady Yellow Jackets had many impressive individual performances. Senior Marisa Scaletta had a game-changing performance for the team. After a slow start and adjustments to the weather conditions, Scaletta delivered an eagle 3 on the par-5 sixth hole, replenishing the team's confidence. She helped secure the win and took home the individual champion title with a 1-under-par 71. Yellow Jackets junior Serra Erlenkeuser and sophomore Sierra Schulze joined the top five, giving T.L. Hanna three players among the state's elite; and

 

Whereas, in a sport that demands precision and focus as well as physical and mental strength, Head Coach Gabby Kitts and her staff utilized their experience and passion for the sport to mold a championship-caliber team. The lessons they have instilled in their golfers will have great life-long impact, shaping them into well-rounded individuals on and off the course; and

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina General Assembly value the pride and recognition that the T.L. Hanna girls golf team has brought to their school and community and look forward to following their continued achievements in the days to come. Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives, the Senate concurring:

 

That the members of the South Carolina General Assembly, by this resolution, congratulate the T.L. Hanna girls golf team and coaches on their impressive win of the 2025-2026 South Carolina High School League AAAAA Division II State Championship title and applaud them on a fantastic season.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to T.L. Hanna Principal Walter Mayfield and Head Coach Gabby Kitts.

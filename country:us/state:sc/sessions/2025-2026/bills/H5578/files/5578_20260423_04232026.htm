A house RESOLUTION

 

to recognize and honor green energy biofuel llc for its exceptional contributions to the state of south carolina in advancing sustainable waste management, economic development, and environmental stewardship, and to commend the company for its continued growth and leadership in the renewable energy supply chain.  

 

Whereas, Green Energy Biofuel LLC, a South Carolina-based company headquartered in Fairfield County, has, for more than eighteen years, distinguished itself as a leader in the collection, processing, and upcycling of fats, oils, and grease and other organic waste streams; and

 

Whereas, the company has built a reputation as one of the largest and most advanced biofuel feedstock processing operations on the East Coast, with the capacity to process more than one hundred million pounds annually while serving thousands of commercial and industrial customers throughout the Southeastern United States; and

 

Whereas, Green Energy Biofuel plays a critical role in supporting the renewable diesel and sustainable aviation fuel industries by supplying high-quality recycled feedstocks, contributing to domestic energy security and reducing reliance on foreign resources; and

 

Whereas, the company's innovative approach diverts millions of pounds of waste from landfills each year, transforming organic materials into valuable commodities, including biofuel feedstocks and compost, and advancing a circular economy; and

 

Whereas, Green Energy Biofuel operates multiple processing facilities across South Carolina and neighboring states, including significant operations in Warrenville, Elgin, and Winnsboro, contributing to regional economic growth, employing more than forty full-time employees with comprehensive benefits in South Carolina, and investing millions of dollars in infrastructure across its facilities; and

 

Whereas, the company has created meaningful employment opportunities while fostering a strong workplace culture, earning recognition as one of the "Best Places to Work" in South Carolina; and

 

Whereas, Green Energy Biofuel has received numerous honors, including the "SC Ambassador of Economic Development" award presented by then Governor Nikki Haley, the South Carolina Department of Health and Environmental Control's "Business of the Year," the "Innovator of the Year" award from the South Carolina Manufacturing Alliance, and the "Recycler of the Year" award from the Carolina Recycling Association, all of which reflect its commitment to environmental excellence; and

 

Whereas, the leadership of Green Energy Biofuel continues to invest in innovation, expansion, and strategic partnerships to further strengthen South Carolina's leadership in sustainable waste management and renewable energy solutions; and

 

Whereas, the company serves as a model of how private enterprise can successfully align economic growth with environmental responsibility, benefiting both local communities and the global environment.  Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:

 

That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor Green Energy Biofuel LLC for its exceptional contributions to the State of South Carolina in advancing sustainable waste management, economic development, and environmental stewardship, and commend the company for its continued growth and leadership in the renewable energy supply chain.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the leadership team of Green Energy Biofuel LLC.

A house RESOLUTION TO CONGRATULATE SENIOR DAYA ROSS OF WREN HIGH SCHOOL IN ANDERSON COUNTY ON CAPTURING THE 2025 SOUTH CAROLINA HIGH SCHOOL LEAGUE CLASS AAAA INDIVIDUAL STATE CHAMPIONSHIP IN GIRLS CROSS COUNTRY AND TO WISH HER MUCH CONTINUED SUCCESS IN HER UPCOMING COLLEGIATE CAREER as a CROSS COUNTRY athlete. Whereas, the House of Representatives is pleased to learn that on November 15, 2025, Wren High School senior Daya Ross won the South Carolina High School League Individual State Championship in Class AAAA girls cross country; and Whereas, dominating the course in Aiken, Daya Ross captured the state championship by more than 31 seconds with an incredible time of 18:03.64 and so led Wren to a fourth-place finish overall. For her outstanding performance this season, Daya was named Region Runner of the Year and was tapped for All-State; and Whereas, having committed to Catawba College in 2025, Daya Ross will continue her running career at that respected institution. After an illustrious high school career, she's looking forward to making the jump to the NCAA (Division 2); and Whereas, Daya gives full credit to Coach Mark Wilson at Wren High for the excellent training she has received, to her family for their strong support, and to God for enabling her to run calmly and for his glory; and Whereas, the House takes great pride in saluting Daya Ross on taking home the girls individual cross country state title and for the exceptional sports record she has compiled thus far. The members look with interest to hearing of the future successes of this talented athlete. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, congratulate senior Daya Ross of Wren High School in Anderson County on capturing the 2025 South Carolina High School League Class AAAA Individual State Championship in girls cross country and wish her much continued success in her upcoming collegiate career as a cross country athlete. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Daya Ross. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 14, 2026 at 04:22 PM

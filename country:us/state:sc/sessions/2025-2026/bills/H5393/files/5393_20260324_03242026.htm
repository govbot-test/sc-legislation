A house RESOLUTION

 

To recognize and celebrate the grand opening of The Chesnee Faith Home Resale store on March 26, 2026.

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives were pleased to hear that on March 26, 2026, Faith Home Christian Recovery will be hosting the grand opening of their new Chesnee Resale Store.

 

Whereas, Faith Home Christian Recovery is a Christian rehabilitation, home, and recovery program for both men and women who are struggling alcoholics and drug addicts. Faith Home was established in 1966 by Danny W. Barnes, Aline "Mama" Barnes, and Rex McCravy; and

 

Whereas, Faith Home provides a no-cost, 8-week residential, and Christ-centered 12-step recovery program to those seeking freedom from alcoholism and drug addiction. The recovery program is based on the fundamental belief that the solution to an individual's problems as the result of alcohol and drug addiction can be found through faith in God and the application of a program of recovery. Their mission is simple, a disciplined approach to helping residents achieve a balanced life of recovery, centered in the word of God, through healthy living, responsible work, and serving others. They live by the scripture Luke 1:37, "For with God nothing will be impossible"; and

 

Whereas, Faith Home Christian Recovery is proud to operate six resale stores throughout Greenwood, Abbeville, and Spartanburg counties. They offer a wide selection of gently used clothing, furniture, household items, toys, and more, all donated by the generous communities they serve. Revenue generated from the sale of donations through the Faith Home Resale stores supports every Faith Home program. By shopping at or donating to these stores, people are directly supporting the daily operations of the residential recovery facilities, keeping the eight-week program free to all who need it; and

 

Whereas, each sale helps to provide food, housing, counseling, and spiritual guidance for those who are ready to break free from addiction and reclaim their futures. Each Faith Home Resale Store provides the community with a meaningful way to give back, while playing a key role in transforming lives; and

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives value the work that the Faith Home Christian Recovery Stores do to not only help provide people with the free 8-week recovery program, but also their work to better the communities around them. Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:

 

That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and celebrate the grand opening of the Chesnee Faith Home Resale Store on March 26, 2026.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Faith Home Christian Recovery.

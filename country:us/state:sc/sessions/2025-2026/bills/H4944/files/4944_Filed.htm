A house RESOLUTION

 

To recognize and honor the members of the york county GOP, York County Republican Women, Clover / Lake Wylie Republican Women, and Republican Women rising for their leadership in Grassroots organizing and their commitment to traditional family values, the protection of women and girls, and parental rights in education and medicine.

 

Whereas, the York County GOP, York County Republican Women, Clover / Lake Wylie Republican Women, and Republican Women Rising have demonstrated strong leadership and dedication to faith, family, personal responsibility, and limited government; and

 

Whereas, members of these organizations have devoted substantial time and effort to grassroots engagement, voter education, and civic participation throughout York County and South Carolina; and

 

Whereas, these organizations have been leaders in advancing traditional family values and advocating for the protection, privacy, and safety of women and girls; and

 

Whereas, these organizations have consistently defended parental rights, affirming that parents are the primary decision-makers in matters of education and medical care for their children; and

 

Whereas, through their service and leadership, these organizations have strengthened conservative civic engagement and contributed to the preservation of constitutional principles in South Carolina. Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:

 

That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor the members of the York County GOP, York County Republican Women, Clover / Lake Wylie Republican Women, and Republican Women Rising for their leadership in grassroots organizing and their commitment to traditional family values, the protection of women and girls, and parental rights in education and medicine.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to representatives of the York County GOP, York County Republican Women, Clover / Lake Wylie Republican Women, and Republican Women Rising.

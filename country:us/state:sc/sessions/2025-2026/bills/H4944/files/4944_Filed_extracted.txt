Sessions, Guffey, King, Ligon, Martin, Moss, Pope and TerribileDocument Path: LC-0487SA-EB26.docxIntroduced in the House on January 15, 2026Adopted by the House on January 15, 2026Summary: York County RepublicansHISTORY OF LEGISLATIVE ACTIONS Date Body Action Description with journal page number 1/15/2026 House Introduced and adopted (House Journal-page 10) View the latest legislative information at the websiteVERSIONS OF THIS BILL01/15/2026 A house RESOLUTION To recognize and honor the members of the york county GOP, York County Republican Women, Clover / Lake Wylie Republican Women, and Republican Women rising for their leadership in Grassroots organizing and their commitment to traditional family values, the protection of women and girls, and parental rights in education and medicine. Whereas, the York County GOP, York County Republican Women, Clover / Lake Wylie Republican Women, and Republican Women Rising have demonstrated strong leadership and dedication to faith, family, personal responsibility, and limited government; and Whereas, members of these organizations have devoted substantial time and effort to grassroots engagement, voter education, and civic participation throughout York County and South Carolina; and Whereas, these organizations have been leaders in advancing traditional family values and advocating for the protection, privacy, and safety of women and girls; and Whereas, these organizations have consistently defended parental rights, affirming that parents are the primary decision-makers in matters of education and medical care for their children; and Whereas, through their service and leadership, these organizations have strengthened conservative civic engagement and contributed to the preservation of constitutional principles in South Carolina. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor the members of the York County GOP, York County Republican Women, Clover / Lake Wylie Republican Women, and Republican Women Rising for their leadership in grassroots organizing and their commitment to traditional family values, the protection of women and girls, and parental rights in education and medicine. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to representatives of the York County GOP, York County Republican Women, Clover / Lake Wylie Republican Women, and Republican Women Rising. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 15, 2026 at 11:21 AM

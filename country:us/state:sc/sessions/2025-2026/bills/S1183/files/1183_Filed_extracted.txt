Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate are pleased to recognize Rafting Creek Elementary School for its service to the Rembert community and to the people and the State of South Carolina; and Whereas, located in Rembert, Rafting Creek Elementary School is a Title I school that houses over one hundred students in pre-kindergarten through fifth grade. For over eighty-nine years, the school has provided quality education for all students in an environment where children feel safe and enjoy learning; and Whereas, Rafting Creek Elementary School, home of the Lions, aims to enable all students to master and exceed grade-level expectations as outlined by the South Carolina College and Career Ready Standards. The school provides its students with equitable learning experiences that will help them become creative thinkers and problem solvers who will produce innovative ideas in the future; and Whereas, Rafting Creek Elementary School has flourished under the guidance of its principal, Anita Hunter; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate greatly appreciate the dedication and commitment that Rafting Creek Elementary School has shown in serving the people and the State of South Carolina.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate: That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, recognize and honor Rafting Creek Elementary School for its service to the Rembert community and to the people and the State of South Carolina. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the principal of Rafting Creek Elementary School, Anita Hunter. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 7, 2026 at 11:22 AM

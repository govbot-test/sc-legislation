A house RESOLUTION TO EXPRESS THE PROFOUND SORROW OF THE MEMBERS OF THE SOUTH CAROLINA HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES UPON THE PASSING OF Timothy Furman Touchberry and EXTEND THEIR DEEPEST SYMPATHY TO his LARGE AND LOVING FAMILY AND His MANY FRIENDS. Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives were saddened to learn of the death of Timothy Furman Touchberry at the age of seventy-four on February 6, 2025; and Whereas, born on September 1, 1950, he was the son of Furman and Esther Touchberry, and he graduated from Stall High School. In preparation for his lifework in education and athletics, he earned a bachelor's degree from the Baptist College at Charleston and a master's degree from The Citadel; and Whereas, Mr. Touchberry began his career teaching and coaching at Stall High School. He spent fifteen years at Summerville High School, inspiring countless students with his commitment, kindness, and steady leadership. When Fort Dorchester High School opened, he was called to become the athletic director, establishing a culture founded in integrity, excellence, and dedication to sportsmanship; and Whereas, together with his beloved wife of fifty-four years, Deborah Jantzen Touchberry, he reared three fine children: Julie, Russ, and Reid. His children blessed him with the affection of six adoring grandchildren: Sage, Beckham, Jake, Amara, Rand, and Orion, whose lives he enriched with his wisdom, warmth, and unwavering love; and Whereas, a man of faith, integrity, and compassion, Mr. Touchberry inspired the lives of so many, guiding future leaders in the classrooms and on the athletic fields and courts; and Whereas, he enjoyed going out on the boat fishing, shrimping, and hunting for the perfect bottle. He will be remembered for lovingly serving his family and his community so dear to him; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives are grateful for the life and legacy of Timothy Touchberry and for the example of service and excellence he set for all who knew him. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, express their profound sorrow upon the passing of Timothy Furman Touchberry and extend their deepest sympathy to his large and loving family and his many friends. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the family of Timothy Furman Touchberry. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 18, 2025 at 12:51 PM

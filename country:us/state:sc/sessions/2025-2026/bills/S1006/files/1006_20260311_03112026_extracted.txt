A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY AMENDING SECTION 4-10-330, RELATING TO THE CAPITAL PROJECTS SALES TAX, SO AS TO DESIGNATE GREENSPACE AND GREENBELT PROGRAMS AS A QUALIFYING PROJECT. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 4-10-330(A)(1)(i) of the S.C. Code is amended to read: (i) greenspace and greenbelt programs; (j) any combination of the projects described in subitems (a) through (h)(i) of this item; SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 11, 2026 at 01:32 PM

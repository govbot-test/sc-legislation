A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY AMENDING SECTION 12-6-3790, RELATING TO THE EDUCATIONAL CREDIT FOR EXCEPTIONAL NEEDS CHILDREN'S FUND, SO AS TO EXPAND THE DEFINITION OF "EXCEPTIONAL NEEDS CHILD" TO INCLUDE A CHILD WHO IS IN FOSTER CARE. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 12-6-3790(A)(2) of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: (c) who is in foster care. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor and applies to income tax years after 2024. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on December 06, 2024 at 11:23 AM

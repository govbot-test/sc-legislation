2025-2026 Bill 4092 Text of Previous Version (Feb. 25, 2025) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Bill 4092Indicates Matter StrickenIndicates New Matter(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) A joint Resolution TO PROVIDE A SALES TAX EXEMPTION DURING THE MONTH OF JULY 2025 FOR HANDGUNS, RIFLES, SHOTGUNS, AND AMMUNITION. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  The gross proceeds of sales or the sales price of new or used handguns, as defined pursuant to Section 16-23-10(1), rifles, shotguns, and ammunition, sold during the month of July 2025, are exempted from the taxes imposed by Chapter 36, Title 12 of the S.C. Code. SECTION 2.  This joint resolution takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 25, 2025 at 01:03 PM

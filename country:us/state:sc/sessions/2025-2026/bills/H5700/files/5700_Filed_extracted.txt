Whereas, Grant has served with distinction as a page for the South Carolina House of Representatives since 2024 and was named head page for the current session; and Whereas, a native of Irmo, Grant is the son of Kevin and April Hunter. A 2024 graduate of Spring Hill High School, he recently completed his sophomore year at the Darla Moore School of Business at the University of South Carolina, where he has earned Dean's List honors every semester; and Whereas, a highly motivated and driven individual, Grant has taken charge and fully immersed himself in campus life and distinguished himself through leadership and service. He was elected to USC's Student Government, where he served in the 117th Student Senate. As a morale team member for the Dance Marathon at USC, he helped raise nearly $800,000 for Palmetto Prisma Health Children's Hospital. Since August 2025, he has served as a resident assistant for Bates House, and he recently earned a place on the 2026-2027 USC Co-Ed Cheer Team; and Whereas, Grant is deeply committed to his faith and is an active member of Midtown Fellowship, where he faithfully serves in a leadership role and strives to encourage and positively influence those around him; and Whereas, through his dedication, leadership, and enthusiasm for the institution of South Carolina state government, Grant has demonstrated exemplary service and commitment to the citizens of this State. His professionalism, work ethic, and respect for the legislative process reflect great credit upon himself, his family, and the South Carolina House of Representatives; and Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives takes great pleasure in celebrating the outstanding achievements, dedicated service, strong character, and leadership that Grant Hunter has demonstrated, and look forward to following his continued success as he makes a meaningful impact on his community, his university, and the State of South Carolina. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor Grant Hunter for his dedicated service as a House page since 2024 and congratulate him on being named head page for the current legislative session. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Grant Hunter. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 13, 2026 at 2:35 PM

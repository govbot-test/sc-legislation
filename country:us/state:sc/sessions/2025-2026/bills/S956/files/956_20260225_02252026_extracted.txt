A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY AMENDING SECTION 12-36-90, RELATING TO THE DEFINITION OF GROSS PROCEEDS OF SALES, SO AS TO EXCLUDE AMOUNTS PAID BY STATE AND LOCAL GOVERNMENTS FOR THE EMERGENCY SERVICES IP NETWORK. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 12-36-90(2) of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: (m) amounts paid for contracts for services entered into by the State or political subdivisions thereof, for Emergency Services IP Network, also known as ESInet, in support of Next Generation 911 in South Carolina. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect July 1, 2026. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 25, 2026 at 02:40 PM

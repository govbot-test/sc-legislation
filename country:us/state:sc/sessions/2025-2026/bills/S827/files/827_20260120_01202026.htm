A senate RESOLUTION

 

TO CONGRATULATE Bob ziembicki UPON THE OCCASION OF HIS RETIREMENT as president of the sUN cITY cLEAR lAKE rEPUBLICAN cLUB, TO COMMEND HIM FOR HIS eight YEARS OF DEDICATED SERVICE, AND TO WISH HIM MUCH HAPPINESS AND FULFILLMENT IN THE YEARS AHEAD.

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate are pleased to recognize Bob Ziembecki upon the occasion of his retirement as the president of the Sun City Republican Club on February 14, 2026; and

 

Whereas, Mr. Ziembicki became a member of the Sun City Republican Club in November 2015 and has served as president since January 1, 2018. Under his tenure, the club grew to around eight hundred members, with monthly meetings normally having over one hundred members in attendance; and

 

Whereas, he administered the collection of dues, which raised over fifteen thousand dollars in the last four years for Lancaster food banks, first responders, and charitable organizations, such as the Lancaster Children's Home, Sherrif's Office, and Coroner's Office. In 2025 the Club's entire donation, approximately three thousand five hundred dollars, went to Samaritan's Purse for those in Asheville, North Carolina who were devastated by Hurricane Helene; and

 

Whereas, Mr. Ziembicki's greatest accomplishment as president was inviting notable local, state, and national Republican candidates, as well as elected officials to Sun City. He cultivated strong relationships with these figures and increased the notoriety of the club; and

 

Whereas, Mr. Ziembicki has also been active in the Sun City community outside of his work with the Republican club. He served on the Sun City Clear Lake Board of Directors for one year as secretary, three months as president, and one month as director; and

 

Whereas, Mr. Ziembicki and will spend his retirement with his wife of forty-seven years, Christine, and their dog, Lucky, serving as a realtor, golfing, and spending time at the beach in Ocean Isle, North Carolina; and

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina Senate appreciate the passion and dedication that Bob Ziembicki has shown in serving the people and the State of South Carolina.  Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the Senate:

 

That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, congratulate Bob Ziembicki upon the occasion of his retirement as president of the Sun City Clear Lake Republican Club, commend him for his eight years of dedicated service, and wish him much happiness and fulfillment in the years ahead.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Bob Ziembicki.

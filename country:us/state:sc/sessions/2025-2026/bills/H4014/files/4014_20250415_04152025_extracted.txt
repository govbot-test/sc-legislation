Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) Recalled April 15, 2025 H. 4014 Introduced by Rep. Bustos L. Printed 4/15/25--S. Read the first time March 26, 2025 ________ A bill TO ABOLISH THE CONSTITUENT DISTRICTS OF CHARLESTON COUNTY SCHOOL DISTRICT AND THEIR RESPECTIVE BOARDS OF TRUSTEES AND TO DELEGATE THE POWERS DEVOLVED UPON THE TRUSTEES OF THE CONSTITUENT DISTRICTS BY ACT 340 OF 1967, AS AMENDED, TO THE BOARD OF TRUSTEES OF CHARLESTON COUNTY SCHOOL DISTRICT. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Notwithstanding another provision of law, the constituent districts of Charleston County School District and their respective boards of trustees are abolished effective July 1, 2025. On July 1, 2025, the constituent districts cease to exist and from that date, all of the powers, duties, and functions now devolved by Act 340 of 1967, as amended, upon the boards of trustees of the constituent districts are assumed and exercised by the Board of Trustees of Charleston County School District. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on April 15, 2025 at 05:00 PM

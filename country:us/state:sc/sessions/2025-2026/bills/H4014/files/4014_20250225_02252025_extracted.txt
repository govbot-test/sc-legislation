Read the first time February 13, 2025 ________ The CHARLESTON DELEGATION To whom was referred a Bill (H. 4014) to abolish the constituent districts of Charleston County School District and their respective boards of trustees and to delegate the powers devolved upon the trustees, etc., respectfully Report: That they have duly and carefully considered the same, and recommend that the same do pass: THOMAS HARTNETT for the Delegation. _______ A bill TO ABOLISH THE CONSTITUENT DISTRICTS OF CHARLESTON COUNTY SCHOOL DISTRICT AND THEIR RESPECTIVE BOARDS OF TRUSTEES AND TO DELEGATE THE POWERS DEVOLVED UPON THE TRUSTEES OF THE CONSTITUENT DISTRICTS BY ACT 340 OF 1967, AS AMENDED, TO THE BOARD OF TRUSTEES OF CHARLESTON COUNTY SCHOOL DISTRICT. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Notwithstanding another provision of law, the constituent districts of Charleston County School District and their respective boards of trustees are abolished effected July 1, 2025. On July 1, 2025, the constituent districts cease to exist and from that date, all of the powers, duties, and functions now devolved by Act 340 of 1967, as amended, upon the boards of trustees of the constituent districts are assumed and exercised by the Board of Trustees of Charleston County School District. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 25, 2025 at 04:28 PM

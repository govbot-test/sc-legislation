To whom was referred a Bill (H. 3731) to amend the South Carolina Code of Laws by repealing Section 2 of Act 201 of 2024 so as to remove a sunset provision concerning the authority of special purpose, etc., respectfully

Report:

That they have duly and carefully considered the same, and recommend that the same do pass:

 

W. NEWTON for Committee.

A bill

 

TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS by REPEALing SECTION 2 OF ACT 201 OF 2024 SO AS TO REMOVE A SUNSET PROVISION CONCERNING THE AUTHORITY OF SPECIAL PURPOSE DISTRICTS TO OWN, DISPOSE, ACQUIRE, PURCHASE, HOLD, USE, LEASE, CONVEY, SELL, TRANSFER, OR OTHERWISE DISPOSE OF PROPERTY.

 

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina:

 

SECTION 1.  Section 2 of Act 201 of 2024 is repealed.

 

SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor.

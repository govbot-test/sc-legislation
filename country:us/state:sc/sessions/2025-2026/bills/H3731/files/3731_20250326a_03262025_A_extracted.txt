Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) Committee Report March 26, 2025 H. 3731 Introduced by Rep. Bernstein S. Printed 3/26/25--H. Read the first time January 15, 2025 ________ The committee on House Judiciary To whom was referred a Bill (H. 3731) to amend the South Carolina Code of Laws by repealing Section 2 of Act 201 of 2024 so as to remove a sunset provision concerning the authority of special purpose, etc., respectfully Report: That they have duly and carefully considered the same, and recommend that the same do pass: W. NEWTON for Committee. _______ A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS by REPEALing SECTION 2 OF ACT 201 OF 2024 SO AS TO REMOVE A SUNSET PROVISION CONCERNING THE AUTHORITY OF SPECIAL PURPOSE DISTRICTS TO OWN, DISPOSE, ACQUIRE, PURCHASE, HOLD, USE, LEASE, CONVEY, SELL, TRANSFER, OR OTHERWISE DISPOSE OF PROPERTY. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 2 of Act 201 of 2024 is repealed. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 26, 2025 at 07:35 PM

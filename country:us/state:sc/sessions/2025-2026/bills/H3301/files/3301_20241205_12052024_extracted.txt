A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY ADDING ARTICLE 17 TO CHAPTER 1, TITLE 1 SO AS TO PROVIDE THAT ANY ESTABLISHMENT THAT HOLDS A RETAIL LICENSE TO DO BUSINESS IN THIS STATE MUST ACCEPT CASH PAYMENTS FOR TRANSACTIONS. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Chapter 1, Title 1 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: Article 17 Cash Payment Section 1-1-1110.  Any establishment that holds a retail license to do business in this State must accept cash payments for transactions. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on December 06, 2024 at 11:16 AM

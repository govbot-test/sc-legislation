Read the first time March 27, 2025 ________ A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY ADDING SECTION 60-2-60 SO AS TO PROVIDE FOR THE REMOVAL OF CERTAIN PUBLIC RECORDS. Amend Title To Conform Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Article 1, Chapter 2, Title 30 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: Section 30-2-60. Eviction filings and records made pursuant to Section 27-40-10 or 27-37-10, including those concluded by Orders of Eviction or Writ of Ejectment, cases resolved by settlement, or resolved by subsequent payment for the judgment that satisfied any debt that include personal information of a defendant, must be removed from the public index and any publicly accessible files available for public record five years after the final disposition or filing if no disposition is recorded. Court records more than five years old must be automatically removed from public index records specified herein. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect on January 1, 2027. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on April 23, 2026 at 09:02 PM

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives are pleased to learn that the Woodmont High School girls volleyball team claimed the 2025 South Carolina Class AAAAA, Division 2 State Championship title at the state tournament held at Dreher High School in Columbia on Thursday, November 6, 2025; and Whereas, to the delight of their faithful fans, the Woodmont Lady Wildcats defeated a fine, competitive field of rivals and swept the North Myrtle Beach High School Chiefs in a thrilling five-set match to secure the program's first state championship title and the school's first state championship in any sport since 1979; and Whereas, in a sport demanding exceptional precision, coordination, and teamwork, Head Coach Haleigh Horgan and her dedicated coaching staff skillfully developed a championship-caliber team, instilling in these student athletes discipline, perseverance, and leadership qualities that will serve them well both on and off the court; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives commend the Woodmont volleyball players for the pride and distinction they have brought to their school and community and look forward with confidence to their continued success in the years ahead.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor the Woodmont High School girls varsity volleyball team, coaches, and school officials for an outstanding season and congratulate them upon winning the 2025 South Carolina Class AAAAA, Division 2 State Championship title. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Principal Melissa Patterson, Athletic Director Chris Carter, and Coach Haleigh Horgan.

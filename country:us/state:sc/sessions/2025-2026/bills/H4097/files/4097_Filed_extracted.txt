Smith, Spann-Wilder, Stavrinakis, Taylor, Teeple, Terribile, Vaughan, Weeks, Wetmore, White, Whitmire, Wickensimer, Williams, Willis, Wooten and YowDocument Path: LC-0093HA-JAH25.docxIntroduced in the House on February 26, 2025Adopted by the House on February 26, 2025Summary: Big Dave's CheesesteaksHISTORY OF LEGISLATIVE ACTIONS Date Body Action Description with journal page number 2/26/2025 House Introduced and adopted (House Journal-page 12) View the latest legislative information at the websiteVERSIONS OF THIS BILL02/26/2025 A house RESOLUTION TO CONGRATULATE BIG DAVE'S CHEESESTEAKS ON THE GRAND OPENING OF THEIR FIRST FRANCHISE LOCATION IN SOUTH CAROLINA. Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is pleased to learn that Big Dave's Cheesesteaks will celebrate its grand opening in Columbia; and Whereas, the original location of Big Dave's Cheesesteaks, which was housed in a seven hundred square-foot gas station in Dunwoody, Georgia, rose to fame when Philadelphia native and popular actress, Eve, visited the location for a taste of home while filming in Atlanta; and Whereas, Big Dave's Cheesesteaks, founded on a commitment to quality, authenticity, and exceptional service, has earned national acclaim as one of the premier destinations for authentic cheesesteaks, bringing people together through the love of great food; and Whereas, the grand opening of the first franchise location of Big Dave's Cheesesteaks in South Carolina marks a significant milestone in the company's growth, and is a testament to the entrepreneurial spirit that drives success in our State; and Whereas, this establishment will not only offer delicious, high-quality food, but will also foster job creation, economic development, and provide a welcoming space for all to enjoy; and Whereas, South Carolina proudly supports businesses that embody dedication, perseverance, and a commitment to excellence, recognizing that their success contributes to the prosperity of our people and the strength of our local economy; and Whereas, the members of the House of Representatives take great pleasure in congratulating Big Dave's Cheesesteaks on its grand opening in Columbia and encourage all citizens to celebrate this momentous occasion by supporting this outstanding business, enjoying its signature cheesesteaks, and embracing the spirit of entrepreneurship that defines our great State.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, congratulate Big Dave's Cheesesteaks on the grand opening of the first franchise location in South Carolina. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Big Dave's Cheesesteaks. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 26, 2025 at 11:05 AM

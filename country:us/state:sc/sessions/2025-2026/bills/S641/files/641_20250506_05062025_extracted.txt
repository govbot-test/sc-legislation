A senate RESOLUTION TO CONGRATULATE South Florence High School in florence school district 1 FOR winning the 2024-25 Palmetto's Finest Award. Whereas, the South Carolina Senate is pleased to learn that South Florence High School in Florence School District 1 has won the 2024-25 Palmetto's Finest award; and Whereas, since 1978, the South Carolina Association of School Administrators has recognized a few schools that offer excellent instruction and outstanding leaders, augmented by strong family and community involvement and a supportive business community. The Palmetto's Finest award honors schools with exceptional student achievement, instruction programs, professional learning communities, and school culture; and Whereas, the Palmetto's Finest award winners exemplify innovation across South Carolina. They set the standard of excellence by cultivating vibrant school environments with high expectations and providing numerous opportunities for academic enrichment; and Whereas, South Florence High School is a winner of this award in the high school category; and Whereas, the Senate is grateful for South Florence High School's dedicated service to the people and the State of South Carolina and takes great pleasure in saluting the school for receiving the prestigious 2024-25 Palmetto's Finest award.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate: That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, congratulate South Florence High School in Florence School District 1 for winning the 2024-25 Palmetto's Finest award. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the principal of South Florence High School, Shand Josey. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 06, 2025 at 12:10 PM

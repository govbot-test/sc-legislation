Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is pleased to learn that Chad Sarvis, Aynor High School head baseball coach, was named Class AAA State Baseball Coach of the Year for 2026 by the South Carolina Baseball Coaches Association (SCBCA); and Whereas, Coach Sarvis has dedicated so much to Blue Jacket baseball and to the players he coaches each season. His leadership, hard work, and commitment to the program have made a lasting impact on so many student athletes; and Whereas, further, his own athletic experience has developed in him the skill, strength, strategy, and quickness that now serve him so well in his capacity as a coach. Day by day, he pays it forward by developing these qualities in his student athletes; and Whereas, giving credit where credit is due, Coach Sarvis is quick to praise Aynor High School's administrators, faculty members and staff, students, parents, and community fans for the support and encouragement that enable the baseball program to shine; and Whereas, grateful for his excellence and dedication as coach, teacher, and life mentor, the members of the House of Representatives take great pleasure in saluting Chad Sarvis of Aynor High on receiving the coveted SCBCA Class AAA State Baseball Coach of the Year award. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, congratulate Chad Sarvis, Aynor High School head baseball coach, on being named 2026 Class AAA State Baseball Coach of the Year by the South Carolina Baseball Coaches Association. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Head Coach Chad Sarvis of Aynor High School. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on June 25, 2026 at 5:46 PM

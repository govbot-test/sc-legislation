A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY AMENDING SECTION 27-30-130, RELATING TO THE ENFORCEABILITY OF GOVERNING DOCUMENTS, RECORDING REQUIREMENTS, RULES, REGULATIONS, AND AMENDMENTS, SO AS TO REQUIRE CERTAIN HOMEOWNERS ASSOCIATION DOCUMENTS TO BE SUBJECT TO SECTION 30-5-30. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 27-30-130(D) of the S.C. Code is amended to read: (D) The recording of the rules, regulations, bylaws, and amendments to rules and regulations are not subject to the requirements of witnesses and acknowledgements required under Section 30-5-30. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 10, 2026 at 12:46 PM

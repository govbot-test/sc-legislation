A bill TO AMEND THE SOUTH CAROLINA CODE OF LAWS BY AMENDING SECTION 4-10-330, RELATING TO the PURPOSES FOR WHICH PROCEEDS OF THE CAPITAL PROJECT SALES TAX MAY BE USED SO AS TO INCLUDE OPERATIONS FOR EMERGENCY SERVICES. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 4-10-330(A)(1) of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: (j) operations for emergency services including, but not limited to, police, fire, and emergency medical technician services; SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on June 25, 2026 at 05:52 PM

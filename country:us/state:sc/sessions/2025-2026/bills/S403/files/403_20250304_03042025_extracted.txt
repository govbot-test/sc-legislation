2025-2026 Bill 403 Text of Previous Version (Mar. 04, 2025) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Bill 403Indicates Matter StrickenIndicates New Matter(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) A senate RESOLUTION TO CONGRATULATE American Honda Motor Co., Inc. South Carolina Manufacturing FOR winning the 2024 Coolest Thing Made in South Carolina contest. Whereas, the South Carolina Senate is pleased to learn that American Honda Motor Co., Inc. South Carolina Manufacturing has won the 2024 Coolest Thing Made in South Carolina contest, which is run by the South Carolina Manufacturers Alliance; and Whereas, Honda's side-by-side power sports vehicle, the Honda Talon, has earned the title of the Coolest Thing Made in South Carolina after a two-month contest that yielded more than two hundred thousand votes from the public; and Whereas, the Honda Talon 1000R-4 Fox Live Valve, which is manufactured by American Honda Motor Co., Inc. South Carolina Manufacturing in Timmonsville, received the most votes in the final round of voting in the third annual Manufacturing Madness contest hosted by the South Carolina Manufacturers Alliance and presented by BlueCross BlueShield of South Carolina; and Whereas, American Honda Motor Co., Inc. South Carolina Manufacturing began operations in Timmonsville in 1998. As the global source for all Honda side-by-side vehicles, the facility has produced over three million power sports products over the last twenty-five years and employs one thousand associates. The Honda Talon 1000R-4 Fox Live Valve is enjoyed by motocross and outdoor enthusiasts who want superior off-road performance and comfort; and Whereas, the Senate is grateful for Honda's dedicated service to innovation and takes great pleasure in saluting the company for winning the 2024 Coolest Thing Made in South Carolina contest.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate: That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, congratulate American Honda Motor Co., Inc. South Carolina Manufacturing for winning the 2024 Coolest Thing Made in South Carolina contest. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the president and CEO of American Honda Motor Co., Inc. and its South Carolina Manufacturing facility leadership. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 04, 2025 at 12:10 PM

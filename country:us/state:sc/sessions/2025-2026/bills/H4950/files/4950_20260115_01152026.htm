A house RESOLUTION

 

To Celebrate and Honor the Chapin High School Competitive Cheer Squad, its coaches, and school officials for an outstanding season and to congratulate them for winning the 2025 class aaaaa division ii state championship title.

 

Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is pleased to learn that the members of the Chapin High School competitive cheer squad took top honors at the South Carolina High School League Competitive Cheer Championships, held at the Colonial Life Arena on Saturday, November 22, 2025; and

 

Whereas, to the delight of their faithful fans, the Chapin High Lady Eagles defeated a fine field of competitors with a score of 334 points besting their nearest opponent, Catawba Ridge, by 25 points; and

 

Whereas, this win marks the Lady Eagles' fourth win in five seasons, as well as their nineteenth overall state title in program history. This monumental win upholds Chapin High School as the only competitive cheer team to claim titles across all competition divisions; and

 

Whereas, in a sport that requires precision, grace, and strength, Head Coach Vicki Williams and her skilled coaching staff capitalized on their own experience and passion to shape a championship caliber team. Together the lessons they have taught their athletes will remain invaluable throughout life, shaping them into well-rounded individuals both on and off the mats; and

 

Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives value the pride and recognition that the Chapin High School competitive cheerleaders have brought to their school and community. They look forward to following the Lady Eagles and celebrating their future achievements. Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:

 

That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, celebrate and honor the Chapin High School competitive cheer squad, its coaches, and school officials for an outstanding season and congratulate them for winning the 2025 Class AAAAA Division II State Championship title.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Chapin High School Principal Edward Davis Jr. and Head Coach Vicki Williams.

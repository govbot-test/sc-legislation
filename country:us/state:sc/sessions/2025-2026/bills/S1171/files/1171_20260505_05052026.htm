A senate RESOLUTION

 

To recognize and Honor the Power of Prayer Foster Care Initiative, and to Commend the initiative for their important work supporting foster Care efforts in the state.

 

Whereas, children are among our community's most vulnerable and valuable citizens, deserving of safety, stability, and the opportunity to thrive; and

 

Whereas, there is a critical and ongoing need for dedicated foster families to provide safe, nurturing homes, particularly for teenagers and sibling groups who are often at greater risk of prolonged separation and instability; and

 

Whereas, foster parents serve a vital role in supporting children through difficult transitions, yet they require continued encouragement, resources, and community support to effectively meet the needs of those in their care; and

 

Whereas, birth families experiencing crisis and hardship benefit from compassionate support, access to services, and opportunities for healing, with the goal of safe and timely reunification with their children whenever possible; and

 

Whereas, the well-being of children and families is a shared responsibility that calls for coordinated collaboration among public agencies, faith-based organizations, community groups, and concerned citizens; and

 

Whereas, the Power of Prayer Foster Care Initiative seeks to unite individuals and communities in prayer, awareness, and action to strengthen foster care efforts and support children and families in need.  Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the Senate:

 

That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, recognize and honor the Power of Prayer Foster Care Initiative, and commend the initiative for their important work supporting foster care efforts in the State.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the vice president of children's ministries, Miracle Hill Ministries, Brenda L. Parks.

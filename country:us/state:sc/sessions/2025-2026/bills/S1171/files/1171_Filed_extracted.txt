2025-2026 Bill 1171: Power of Prayer Foster Care Initiative - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Download This Bill in Microsoft Word FormatIndicates Matter StrickenIndicates New MatterS. 1171STATUS INFORMATIONSenate ResolutionSponsors: Senator ReichenbachDocument Path: SR-0666KM-AMB26.docxIntroduced in the Senate on May 5, 2026Adopted by the Senate on May 5, 2026Summary: Power of Prayer Foster Care InitiativeHISTORY OF LEGISLATIVE ACTIONS Date Body Action Description with journal page number 5/5/2026 Senate Introduced and adopted (Senate Journal-page 8) View the latest legislative information at the websiteVERSIONS OF THIS BILL05/05/2026 A senate RESOLUTION To recognize and Honor the Power of Prayer Foster Care Initiative, and to Commend the initiative for their important work supporting foster Care efforts in the state. Whereas, children are among our community's most vulnerable and valuable citizens, deserving of safety, stability, and the opportunity to thrive; and Whereas, there is a critical and ongoing need for dedicated foster families to provide safe, nurturing homes, particularly for teenagers and sibling groups who are often at greater risk of prolonged separation and instability; and Whereas, foster parents serve a vital role in supporting children through difficult transitions, yet they require continued encouragement, resources, and community support to effectively meet the needs of those in their care; and Whereas, birth families experiencing crisis and hardship benefit from compassionate support, access to services, and opportunities for healing, with the goal of safe and timely reunification with their children whenever possible; and Whereas, the well-being of children and families is a shared responsibility that calls for coordinated collaboration among public agencies, faith-based organizations, community groups, and concerned citizens; and Whereas, the Power of Prayer Foster Care Initiative seeks to unite individuals and communities in prayer, awareness, and action to strengthen foster care efforts and support children and families in need.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate: That the members of the South Carolina Senate, by this resolution, recognize and honor the Power of Prayer Foster Care Initiative, and commend the initiative for their important work supporting foster care efforts in the State. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the vice president of children's ministries, Miracle Hill Ministries, Brenda L. Parks. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 5, 2026 at 2:00 PM

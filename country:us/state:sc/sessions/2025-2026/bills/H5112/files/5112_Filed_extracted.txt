Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Section 12-37-3150(B) of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: (16) transfers made amongst related descendants who have legal claim to heirs' property, whereby both the grantor and grantee owned an interest in the property prior to the transfer. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 5, 2026 at 11:22 AM

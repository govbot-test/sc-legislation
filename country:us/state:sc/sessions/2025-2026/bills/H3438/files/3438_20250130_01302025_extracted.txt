Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) Committee Report January 30, 2025 H. 3438 Introduced by Reps. Pope and B. Newton S. Printed 1/30/25--H. Read the first time January 14, 2025 ________ The YORK DELEGATION To whom was referred a Joint Resolution (H. 3438) to provide that the General Assembly approves Ordinance Number 3421 adopted on September 7, 2021, by the York County Council to expand the Catawba Indian Reservation, etc., respectfully Report: That they have duly and carefully considered the same, and recommend that the same do pass: HEATH SESSIONS for the Delegation. _______ A joint Resolution TO PROVIDE THAT THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY APPROVES ORDINANCE NUMBER 3421 ADOPTED ON SEPTEMBER 7, 2021, BY THE YORK COUNTY COUNCIL TO EXPAND THE CATAWBA INDIAN RESERVATION, AS REQUESTED BY THE CATAWBA INDIAN NATION. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  The General Assembly approves Ordinance Number 3421 adopted on September 7, 2021, by the York County Council to expand the Catawba Indian Reservation, as requested by the Catawba Indian Nation. SECTION 2.  This joint resolution takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 30, 2025 at 02:40 PM

A joint Resolution

 

To Direct the Department of Administration, the State Fiscal Accountability Authority, or the appropriate agency, to transfer the National Guard armory in the city of Barnwell, South Carolina, to Barnwell County.

 

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina:

 

Notwithstanding Sections 1-11-58 and 25-1-1660 of the S.C. Code, the Department of Administration, the State Fiscal Accountability Authority, or the appropriate agency, is directed to transfer ownership of the National Guard Armory located at 10233 Marlboro Avenue, Barnwell, South Carolina, to Barnwell County.

 

SECTION 1.  This joint resolution takes effect upon approval by the Governor.

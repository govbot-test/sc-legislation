Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) Recalled May 7, 2026 S. 1011 Introduced by Senator Hutto S. Printed 5/7/26--H.                                                                             [SEC 5/12/2026 3:58 PM] Read the first time March 31, 2026 ________ A joint Resolution To Direct the Department of Administration, the State Fiscal Accountability Authority, or the appropriate agency, to transfer the National Guard armory in the city of Barnwell, South Carolina, to Barnwell County. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Notwithstanding Sections 1-11-58 and 25-1-1660 of the S.C. Code, the Department of Administration, the State Fiscal Accountability Authority, or the appropriate agency, is directed to transfer ownership of the National Guard Armory located at 10233 Marlboro Avenue, Barnwell, South Carolina, to Barnwell County. SECTION 2.  This joint resolution takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 12, 2026 at 03:59 PM

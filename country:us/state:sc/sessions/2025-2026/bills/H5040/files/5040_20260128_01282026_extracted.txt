2025-2026 Bill 5040 Text of Previous Version (Jan. 28, 2026) - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Bill 5040Indicates Matter StrickenIndicates New Matter(Text matches printed bills. Document has been reformatted to meet World Wide Web specifications.) A house RESOLUTION TO CONGRATULATE THE MEMBERS OF THE CHESTERFIELD DIXIE YOUTH 8U SOFTBALL TEAM FOR THEIR OUTSTANDING ACHIEVEMENTS AS 2025 STATE CHAMPIONS, TO SALUTE THEM FOR REPRESENTING WELL THE STATE OF SOUTH CAROLINA AT THE 2025 SOFTBALL WORLD SERIES IN LOUISIANA, AND TO COMMEND THESE FINE YOUNG ATHLETES FOR THEIR HARD WORK, DEDICATION, SPORTSMANSHIP, AND EXCEPTIONAL TALENT. Whereas, the members of the Chesterfield Dixie Youth 8U softball team demonstrated extraordinary dedication, perseverance, and teamwork throughout the 2025 softball season, culminating in their achievement as 2025 state champions; and Whereas, through countless hours of practice, disciplined training, and unwavering commitment, these young athletes consistently displayed a strong work ethic and a determination to excel, both individually and collectively; and Whereas, the Chesterfield Dixie Youth 8U softball team's championship performance earned them the honor of representing the State of South Carolina at the 2025 Softball World Series in Louisiana, where they competed against elite teams from across the nation; and Whereas, at both the state and national levels, the team showcased not only exceptional athletic skill and competitive spirit but also outstanding sportsmanship, respect for opponents, and pride in their community; and Whereas, the 2025 Chesterfield Dixie Youth 8U softball team roster included the following players: Emerson Owens (#00), McKinley Pearson (#1), Freya Jacobs (#2), Ryleigh Chavis (#3), Kasey Brock (#5), Brystol Smith (#7), Jayda Rivers (#8), Everly Adams (#10), Adeline Morton (#11), Abrieana Davila (#13), Georgia Burr (#16), and Paityn Griggs (#55); and Whereas, the team was led by a dedicated coaching staff consisting of Barron Pearson, Bruce Chavis, Taylor Griggs, and Stephanie Griggs, whose leadership, instruction, and commitment were instrumental in the team's success; and Whereas, the success of the Chesterfield Dixie Youth 8U softball team reflects positively upon Chesterfield County and the State of South Carolina, serving as an inspiration to other young athletes and demonstrating the values of teamwork, discipline, and perseverance; and Whereas, the accomplishments of these young athletes would not have been possible without the dedicated support of their coaches, families, and others, whose encouragement and guidance played a vital role in the team's success. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, congratulate the members of the Chesterfield Dixie Youth 8U softball team for their outstanding achievements as 2025 state champions, salute them for representing well the State of South Carolina at the 2025 Softball World Series in Louisiana, and commend these fine young athletes for their hard work, dedication, sportsmanship, and exceptional talent. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the members and coaching staff of the Chesterfield Dixie Youth 8U softball team. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on January 28, 2026 at 03:07 PM

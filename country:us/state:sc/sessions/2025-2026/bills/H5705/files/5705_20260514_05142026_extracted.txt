A house RESOLUTION to recognize and honor Jimmy Crosby, a dedicated educator in Richland School District Two for more than three decades and to express the deepest appreciation for his commitment to excellence and to his students. Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives value the work of dedicated teachers in the Palmetto State, like Jimmy Crosby, who labor with devotion to prepare students to take their place in their communities as productive citizens who can lead and participate; and Whereas, Mr. Crosby has served not only as an educator, but also as a mentor and advocate for students, and his impact has been felt across the Richland School District Two community for more than three decades; and Whereas, a native of Orangeburg and proud alumnus of the University of South Carolina, Mr. Crosby devoted thirty-one years of teaching both special education and social studies with compassion, patience, and unwavering commitment; and Whereas, throughout his career, he served students and families at Ridge View High School, Westwood High School, Lake Carolina Elementary School, and Bookman Road Elementary School; and Whereas, known for his steady encouragement and genuine care for others, Mr. Crosby consistently went beyond the classroom to support students academically, emotionally, and personally. His ability to see potential in young people and help them rise to new heights left a lasting impression on countless students over the years; and Whereas, together with his beloved wife of thirty-seven years, Stephanie Crosby, a dedicated employee of Bookman Road Elementary School, he built a life centered around education, service, and family. Together they reared their devoted son, Jordan Crosby, a graduate of Ridge View High School; and Whereas, after a lifetime of service in education, Mr. Crosby will leave a legacy reflected not only in the schools where he taught, but also in the many lives he changed through his kindness, leadership, and belief in the potential of every student; and Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is thankful for the work and dedication of Jimmy Crosby and the beneficial impact that his life and career have had on generations of students who are succeeding because of his care and the value he has placed on their lives. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor Jimmy Crosby, a dedicated educator in Richland School District Two for more than three decades, and express the deepest appreciation for his commitment to excellence and to his students. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Jimmy Crosby. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on May 14, 2026 at 12:42 PM

2025-2026 Bill 805: Pinewood Prep State Champions - South Carolina Legislature Online South Carolina General Assembly126th Session, 2025-2026Download This Bill in Microsoft Word FormatIndicates Matter StrickenIndicates New MatterS. 805STATUS INFORMATIONConcurrent ResolutionSponsors: Senator BennettDocument Path: LC-0226HA-EB26.docxIntroduced in the Senate on January 14, 2026Introduced in the House on January 15, 2026Adopted by the General Assembly on January 15, 2026Summary: Pinewood Prep State ChampionsHISTORY OF LEGISLATIVE ACTIONS Date Body Action Description with journal page number 1/14/2026 Senate Introduced, adopted, sent to House (Senate Journal-page 11) 1/15/2026 House Introduced, adopted, returned with concurrence (House Journal-page 26) View the latest legislative information at the websiteVERSIONS OF THIS BILL01/14/2026 A concurrent RESOLUTION To recognize and honor the Pinewood Preparatory School Football team, coaches, and school officials for a truly remarkable season and to congratulate them on winning the 2025 South Carolina Independent School Association 3A STATE Championship. Whereas, the members of the South Carolina General Assembly are pleased to learn that on November 22, 2025, at Oliver C. Dawson Stadium in Orangeburg, the Pinewood Prep football team captured their second consecutive state championship; and Whereas, to the delight of their passionate fans, the Pinewood Prep Panthers defeated the Wilson Hall Barons by an impressive 42 points, finishing the game 55-13; and Whereas, the Panthers were determined to defend their state title. Despite starting the year with many new faces, the team and staff worked hard leading up to this championship game to develop a cohesive, well-rounded team that had an impressive season record of 10-3; and Whereas, they started the game strong, scoring early, building momentum and confidence. They ended the first half with a score of 42-7, allowing the team to easily take control throughout the second half, dominating the Wilson Hall Barons and winning the state title for a second year in a row; and Whereas, junior quarterback Silas Brawner, who was new to the team this year, made a big impact offensively in the championship game. He tossed five touchdown passes and totaled 331 yards. Other impressive Panther performances included junior wide receiver Lavoris Lucas, who caught three touchdown passes and had 143 yards on eight catches; senior wide receiver Jayden Alexander, who had five receptions for 148 yards and scored twice; junior wide receiver Tre Green, who caught a touchdown pass; senior quarterback Will Watson, who rushed for a score and threw for a touchdown; and junior running back Grayson Salego, who had a rushing touchdown in the fourth quarter; and Whereas, in a sport that requires mental and physical strength, Head Coach DeVonte Holloman and his talented staff utilized their own experience and passion for the sport to craft a championship-caliber team. The lessons they have taught these young athletes will prove invaluable throughout their lives on and off the field; and Whereas, the members of South Carolina General Assembly value the pride and recognition that the Pinewood Prep football team has brought to their school and community. They look forward to following the Panthers and celebrating their future achievements. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring: That the members of the South Carolina General Assembly, by this resolution, recognize and honor the Pinewood Preparatory School football team, coaches, and school officials for a truly remarkable season and congratulate them for winning the 2025 South Carolina Independent School Association 3A State Championship. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Pinewood Preparatory Head of School Daniel Seiden and Head Coach DeVonte Holloman. ----XX--- This web page was last updated on January 14, 2026 at 1:28 PM

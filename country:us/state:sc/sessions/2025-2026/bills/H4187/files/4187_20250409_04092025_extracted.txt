Read the first time March 20, 2025 ________ The LEXINGTON DELEGATION To whom was referred a Bill (H. 4187) to extend the one percent sales tax imposed by Act 378 of 2004, as amended, the Lexington County School District Property Tax Relief Act, for an additional seven, etc., respectfully Report: That they have duly and carefully considered the same, and recommend that the same do pass: M. CASKEY for the Delegation. _______ A bill TO EXTEND THE ONE PERCENT SALES TAX IMPOSED BY ACT 378 OF 2004, AS AMENDED, THE LEXINGTON COUNTY SCHOOL DISTRICT PROPERTY TAX RELIEF ACT, FOR AN ADDITIONAL SEVEN YEARS. Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Pursuant to Section 3(A) of Act 378 of 2004, the one percent special sales and use tax imposed by the Lexington County School District Property Tax Relief Act, and extended by Act 278 of 2018, is extended for an additional seven years. SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon approval by the Governor. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on April 09, 2025 at 09:06 PM

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of South Carolina: SECTION 1.  Chapter 2, Title 12 of the S.C. Code is amended by adding: Section 12-2-150.  Notwithstanding any other provision of law, all questions of law decided by a court, including interpretations of constitutional, statutory, and regulatory provisions, must be made without any deference to any determination or interpretation, whether written or unwritten, that may have been made on the matter by the Department of Revenue.

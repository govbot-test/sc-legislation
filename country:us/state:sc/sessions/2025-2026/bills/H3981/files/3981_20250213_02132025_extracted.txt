A house RESOLUTION to recognize and commend the Honorable Nani Juwara for spearheading The energy and natural resource transformation of the gambia as its minister of Petroleum, Energy and mines. Whereas, it is with great pleasure that the South Carolina House of Representatives recognizes the Honorable Nani Juwara, Minister of Petroleum, Energy and Mines for The Gambia, for being a dynamic and results-driven leader, with a vision for sustainability and economic growth. He has championed strategic policies that are reshaping the country's energy landscape, fostering investments, and ensuring energy security for future generations; and Whereas, under his leadership, The Gambia has launched the Independent Power Producer (IPP) Regional Solar Park, a groundbreaking renewable energy project designed to boost solar capacity and provide affordable electricity to households and businesses; and Whereas, the Honorable Nani Juwara also played a pivotal role in the validation of the Quarrying Regulations 2024, establishing a robust framework for responsible mineral extraction, environmental protection, and compliance with global best practices. His proactive engagement with the Economic Community of West African States (ECOWAS), and international partners, has positioned The Gambia as a key player in West Africa's energy and mining sector; and Whereas, prior to his ministerial appointment, the Honorable Nani Juwara made a lasting impact as managing director of The Gambia National Water and Electricity Company (NAWEC), where he initiated operational reforms that improved efficiency and service delivery nationwide. His strategic vision, coupled with an unwavering commitment to sustainable development, continues to drive The Gambia toward a future of energy independence, economic prosperity, and environmental stewardship; and Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives greatly appreciate the Honorable Nani Juwara's outstanding leadership and the positive changes he has made for The Gambia in Africa's energy landscape. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and commend the Honorable Nani Juwara for spearheading the energy and natural resource transformation of The Gambia as Minister of Petroleum, Energy and Mines. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the Honorable Nani Juwara. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 13, 2025 at 11:24 AM

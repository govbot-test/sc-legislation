A house RESOLUTION

 

TO RECOGNIZE AND HONOR THE FORT MILL HIGH SCHOOL WRESTLING TEAM FOR A STELLAR SEASON AND TO CONGRATULATE THE YELLOW JACKETS ON WINNING THE 2026 CLASS AAAAA DIVISION II STATE CHAMPIONSHIP TITLE.

 

Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is pleased to learn that the members of the Fort Mill High School wrestling team captured the Class AAAAA Division II top prize at this year's state tournament, held at White Knoll High School in Lexington on Wednesday, February 11, 2026; and

 

Whereas, to the cheers of their delighted fans, the Fort Mill Yellow Jackets won with a dominant 52-11 decision against Lugoff-Elgin to claim their fourth consecutive state wrestling crown; and

 

Whereas, moreover, the Yellow Jackets finished the season with a perfect 31-0 record, marking their second straight undefeated season, and this new 2026 championship extended the program's winning streak to 67 consecutive dual matches, the longest active streak in South Carolina; and

 

Whereas, during the finals, Peter Escudero (106 pounds) clinched the team title with a 19-4 technical fall; Lincoln Greene (157 pounds) secured a 16-0 technical fall, ending his career with four championship rings; Luke Van Beynum (120 pounds) recorded a pin in just 18 seconds; and Krystian Villatoro (heavyweight) added a first-period pin to bolster the score; and

 

Whereas, under Head Coach Adam Marullo, the team has lost only three dual matches over the last four years. Following the team championship, Fort Mill also won the 5A Division II Upper State Individual Tournament with 246.5 points, qualifying twelve wrestlers for the individual state tournament. At the individual state tournament, Lincoln Greene was crowned 2026 150 Pounds Class AAAAA State Champion; and

 

Whereas, clearly, Fort Mill has firmly established a wrestling dynasty in the Palmetto State; and

 

Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives appreciates the pride and recognition the Fort Mill wrestlers have brought to their school and community, and the members look forward to following their continued achievements in the days ahead. Go, Yellow Jackets! Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:

 

That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor the Fort Mill High School wrestling team for a stellar season and congratulate the Yellow Jackets on winning the 2026 Class AAAAA Division II State Championship title.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Principal Zachary D. Beam and Head Coach Adam Marullo of Fort Mill High School.

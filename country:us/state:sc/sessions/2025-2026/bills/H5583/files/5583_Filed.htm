A house RESOLUTION

 

TO recognize the congregation, church staff, and Reverend Dale McCoig of Enoree Baptist Church and to congratulate them as they celebrate one hundred seventy-five years of ministry and service to their community.

 

Whereas, Enoree Baptist Church in Travelers Rest was founded on August 30, 1851, by a group of seventeen charter members. A modest log building served the congregation from 1851 until 1859. From its earliest days, the founding members served to ground the church in a strong commitment to faith and community by establishing the clear guiding principles of adherence to the Bible as the sole standard of faith, recognition of Jesus Christ as the head of the church, and a shared responsibility to support one another spiritually and personally; and

 

Whereas, as the church grew, it became part of a broader network of churches, forming the Enoree Baptist Association alongside seven other area churches. The congregation of Enoree Baptist expanded both its physical presence and its outreach efforts with one significant step that introduced a Sunday school which provided structured religious education for members of all ages; and

 

Whereas, the church purchased a bus to transport individuals who otherwise lacked access to attend services, highlighting its commitment to outreach. Beyond its own growth, Enoree Baptist played a role in the development of other congregations, as some of its members helped to establish and support new Baptist churches in surrounding areas; and

 

Whereas, the church focuses outwardly to support for mission work, both within the United States and internationally, demonstrating a commitment to spread its faith beyond its immediate community; and

 

Whereas, education became an increasingly important part of the church's mission in the twentieth century. In 1971-1972, Enoree Baptist Church (EBC) supported a kindergarten program through its Bible School, emphasizing basic academic skills and Christian values which reflected the church's belief in the importance of nurturing both intellectual and spiritual growth from an early age; and

 

Whereas, over the years, this kindergarten program evolved into what is now known as EBC Academy. The academy serves children from eight weeks old through four-year-old kindergarten and continues the original mission of providing foundational education alongside faith-based lessons; and

 

Whereas, Enoree Baptist has developed programs to serve individuals in all stages of life. The Enoree Student Ministry and Enoree Kid's Ministry focus on engaging young people, fostering their spiritual development, and encouraging them to build a relationship with God from a young age. For older members, the church offers the Young at Heart program, which supports senior adults by providing fellowship opportunities and strengthening their faith within church community; and

 

Whereas, the church has maintained a strong emphasis on compassion in the community through its C.A.R.E. program, an initiative which supports and encourages individuals within the church and the broader community, reflecting the founding principle of mutual support among members; and

 

Whereas, a century and three quarters after its founding, Enoree Baptist Church continues to hold firmly to the beliefs that first brought its members together with a clearly defined purpose: "Enoree Baptist Church strives to glorify God by exalting Christ, equipping Christians, and embracing community so that anyone and everyone may experience the encouragement of life in the Spirit"; and

 

Whereas, under the current pastoral leadership of Reverend Dale McCoig, the church continues to grow while staying grounded in its core values. A welcoming place for people from all backgrounds to come together in faith, Enoree Baptist Church stands as a testament to enduring faith. Now, therefore,

 

Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:

 

That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize the congregation, church staff, and Reverend Dale McCoig of Enoree Baptist Church and congratulate them as they celebrate one hundred seventy-five years of ministry and service to their community.

 

Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Reverend Dale McCoig.

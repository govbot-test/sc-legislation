Whereas, Suits and Sneakers is a collaboration between the American Cancer Society and the American Cancer Society Cancer Action Network to leverage the personal experiences, community leadership, and professional excellence of coaches nationwide to increase cancer awareness and promote healthy living through year-round awareness efforts, fundraising activities, and advocacy programs; and Whereas, the American Cancer Society Cancer Action Network empowers advocates across the State to make their voices heard and influence evidence-based public policy change that will reduce the cancer burden on our State; and Whereas, it is estimated that more than thirty-six thousand South Carolinians will be diagnosed with cancer in 2026; and Whereas, cancer patients face countless challenges associated with their diagnoses, including physical, emotional, financial, and day-to-day challenges; and Whereas, as South Carolina continues to navigate through difficult legislation, it is more important than ever to support efforts to combat cancer; and Whereas, not only will effective policies serve to prevent, detect, and treat cancer and save countless lives, but they will significantly reduce the financial strain that this disease places on South Carolinians and our great State; and Whereas, the American Cancer Society Cancer Action Network would like to recognize all cancer survivors, caregivers, and advocates. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, honor cancer patients, survivors, and their families, remember those people who have been lost to cancer, and declare Wednesday, March 4, as "Suits and Sneakers Day" in South Carolina. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Governor Henry McMaster and to the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on February 24, 2026 at 1:37 PM

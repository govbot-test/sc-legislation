Whereas, the West Ashley High School Navy Junior Reserve Officers Training Corps (NJROTC) has continually proven itself to be a beacon of leadership and community spirit, particularly through its involvement in the Annual Thanksgiving Turkey Giveaway and the Annual Christmas Toy Giveaway at the Community Resource Center; and Whereas, this dedicated group of cadets has gone above and beyond the call of duty, exemplifying the core values of service and leadership by volunteering to assist in the distribution of vital resources to over three thousand families in the Lowcountry, and have shown remarkable leadership skills in assisting during these significant community events; and Whereas, their ability to organize efficiently and work collaboratively ensured a smooth distribution process, providing much-needed support to families in need, and this kind of initiative not only highlights their commitment to service, but also cultivates a sense of responsibility and empathy among the cadets; and Whereas, the impact of these West Ashley High School students has been deeply felt within the community. By dedicating their time and energy to the NJROTC, they have brought a measure of joy and relief to countless individuals, especially during the holiday seasons, and their work has been a beam of hope and support, demonstrating the power of collective action and the importance of giving back; and Whereas, their unwavering dedication and willingness to serve have made a significant difference in the lives of many, inspiring others to follow in their footsteps, and the community sincerely appreciates their efforts. The cadets' actions have not only provided immediate assistance, but have also fostered a sense of unity and compassion within the Lowcountry; and Whereas, the House of Representatives is grateful for the West Ashley High School NJROTC's participation in these community events as a shining example of how young leaders can make a meaningful impact, and the members value the cadets' extraordinary service and commitment to their community as something to be celebrated and emulated. Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor the West Ashley High School Navy Junior Reserve Officers Training Corps cadets for their exemplary community service. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to the West Ashley High School Navy Junior Reserve Officers Training Corps. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on April 1, 2026 at 10:58 AM

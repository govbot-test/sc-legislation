A house RESOLUTION to congratulate the northwestern high school football team, coaches, and school officials for an outstanding season and to honor them for winning the south carolina class AAAAA Division II 2025 state championship title. Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives is proud to recognize the Northwestern High School football team for winning the South Carolina Class AAAAA Division II State Championship on December 5, 2025.  This historic win marks the program's second consecutive state championship title; and Whereas, under the visionary leadership of Head Coach Page Wofford and his dedicated coaching staff, the team compiled an impressive overall record of thirteen victories and one defeat, demonstrating discipline, determination, and an unwavering commitment to excellence; and Whereas, to the delight of their loyal and enthusiastic supporters, the Northwestern Trojans defeated an outstanding Irmo High School football team 55-13, in a hard-fought championship contest, a victory that stands as a resounding testament to the team's perseverance, teamwork, and exemplary sportsmanship; and Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives takes great pride in honoring these exceptional student-athletes, who have brought distinction to their school and community through their remarkable performance, and the members look forward with confidence to the team's continued success in the years ahead.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, congratulate the Northwestern High School football team, coaches, and school officials for an outstanding season and honor them for winning the 2025 South Carolina Class AAAAA Division II State Championship title. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Principal Michael Abraham and Coach Page Wofford. This web page was last updated on February 04, 2026 at 10:44 AM

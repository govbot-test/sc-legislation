A house RESOLUTION to recognize and honor the thomas sumter academy junior varsity competition cheer squad, their coaches, and school officials for a superb season and to congratulate them for winning the 2025 south carolina independent school association state championship title. Whereas, the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives are pleased to learn that the Thomas Sumter Academy junior varsity competition cheer squad captured the state title at the 2025 South Carolina Independent School Association (SCISA) Class 3A/4A State Cheer Competition, held in November 2025; and Whereas, to the delight of their faithful fans, the Thomas Sumter Lady Generals defeated a fine field of competitors to secure the 2025 state championship title; and Whereas, twenty-one dedicated student athletes make up the championship team:  Addison Conyers, Adley Boykin, Anna Lumley, Ashlynn Singelton, Charlotte Dorman, Daniellia Barnes, Gabby Tisdale, Huson Laney, Mollie Gray Rogers, Julia Andrade, Jenna Ray, Kaylee Grace Smith, Keira Anderson, Layla Blackmon, Madi DeLa Torre, Reagan Hinson, Remi Yelenic, Risden Dunlap, Zoey Rivers, Maddi Smith, and Hailey Finan; and Whereas, in a sport that requires grace and precision, Coaches Deseree Allen, Jennifer Brazell, and Jessica Reid capitalized on their own athletic experience and training to build a championship team and teach these athletes life principles that will endure well beyond the mats; and Whereas, the South Carolina House of Representatives values the pride and recognition that the Thomas Sumter Academy competitive cheer squad has brought to their school and community, and the members look forward to following their continued achievements in the future.  Now, therefore, Be it resolved by the House of Representatives: That the members of the South Carolina House of Representatives, by this resolution, recognize and honor the Thomas Sumter Academy junior varsity competition cheer squad, their coaches, and school officials for a superb season and congratulate them for winning the 2025 South Carolina Independent School Association State Championship title. Be it further resolved that a copy of this resolution be presented to Principal Ben Herod and Coaches Deseree Allen, Jennifer Brazell, and Jessica Reid. ----XX---- This web page was last updated on March 25, 2026 at 11:39 AM
